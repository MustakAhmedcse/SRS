1. Campaign Overview & Structure
The Banglalink Champions League (BCL) is a monthly and quarterly performance recognition programme for internal field hierarchy and distribution channel stakeholders. It rewards top performers across multiple KPI dimensions using a points-based ranking system. The campaign spans Sep 2025 through Mar 2026 and covers four distinct recipient tiers.
Recipient Tiers
[TABLE]
| Tier | Who | SRF Count | Evaluation Scope |
| Cluster | Cluster-level distributors / territory | 9 | National Top — Best Cluster |
| Region | Regional grouping of clusters | 9 | Best Region per Cluster |
| MDO | Market Development Officers | 5 | National Top 10 MDO |
| ZM / TO / SZM | Zone Manager / Territory Officer / Sr. ZM | 9 | Top 2 per Region / Top 3 per Cluster / National Top 5 |
[/TABLE]
Campaign Periods
[TABLE]
| Period Type | Months Covered | Files |
| Monthly | Sep, Oct, Nov, Dec 2025 | Jan, Feb, Mar 2026 | ~26 SRFs |
| Quarterly | Q4'25 (Oct–Dec 2025) | Q1'26 (Jan–Mar 2026) | ~6 SRFs |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| Note: All 32 SRFs share the same requester (Akrabina Tajwar, Channel Planning) and follow a common structural framework, but differ in KPI composition, scoring weights, eligibility gates, and winner evaluation logic across periods. |
[/TABLE]

3. KPI-01 — Recharge (EV Secondary + SC Primary)
Definition
Recharge achievement is the sum of two components:
EV Secondary (RSO to Retail): Includes R2RT, D2RT, SUD12AGENT, DTR (Manual & Daily Commission from Salescom) and OTF (EV OTF + IRIS OTF), taken as per usual process. Less withdrawal deducted.
SC Primary (BL to DH/Lifting): Primary lifting by distributor from Banglalink warehouse (DD lifting). Less withdrawal deducted.
Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |
| DRC Exclusion | DRC EV Secondary is excluded from recharge achievement in all SRFs |
| Monobrand Exclusion | Monobrand transactions are excluded from all KPI achievements |
| Withdrawal | Less withdrawal applied to both EV Secondary and SC Primary |
| Achievement Basis | EV Tertiary (retailer to subscriber) + SC Primary lifting combined |
| Data Bundle Recharge | Valid i-TOP UP recharge only for Data Pack Upsell |
| Voice Bundle Recharge | Valid i-TOP UP recharge only for Voice Pack Upsell |
| Mix Bundle Recharge | Valid i-TOP UP recharge only for Mix Bundle Upsell |
| Data Source | IT SC + IT BI + DWH |
[/TABLE]
Recharge as a Gate Condition (from Jan 2026)
From Jan 2026, Recharge is also used as a Campaign Eligibility Gate. The minimum gate varies by month:
[TABLE]
| Period | Recharge Gate Threshold |
| Sep–Nov 2025 (Campaign Gate) | Recharge eligibility gate — exact % shared by B2C via VLR |
| Dec 2025 | Campaign Gates: Recharge must meet eligibility threshold |
| Jan–Mar 2026 | Campaign Gate: Recharge 100% of Slab 1 Target |
| Feb–Mar 2026 | Campaign Gate: Recharge 100% of Slab 1 Target (confirmed) |
| Q4'25 Quarterly | 100% of Q4'25 Target for Cluster/Region-level evaluation |
| Q1'26 Quarterly | 95% or 100% of Q1'26 Slab 1 Target (two tiers shown) |
[/TABLE]
4. KPI-02 — Gross Addition (GA)
Definition
GA (Gross Addition) measures new SIM activations. From Nov 2025 it became a primary KPI alongside Recharge.
GA Calculation Rules
[TABLE]
| Rule | Details |
| Connection types included | MNP (Mobile Number Portability) & RYZE connections are counted as GA based on FCD (First Call Date) |
| Disconnected numbers | Excluded from GA achievement in all SRFs |
| DRC exclusion | GA Achievement calculated without DRC in all SRFs |
| Monobrand exclusion | Monobrand GA is excluded from achievement |
| Activation timing | Activation must occur During the Promotion Period |
| Data basis | FCD between activation and latest data up to report generation date (T-6) |
| Products counted | Prepaid; for MNP — all connections |
[/TABLE]
GA: Date-Based Calculation Split (Oct 2025)
[TABLE]
| Special rule for Oct 2025 only: Achievement from 1st–16th October 2025 follows current policy. New calculation criteria applies for GA from 17th October 2025 onwards (example shared in SRF). |
[/TABLE]
Quality GA — Usage Condition
From Nov 2025, GA quality is measured with a usage threshold:
At least 95% of total GA (before disconnection) must have more than BDT 5 usage within 7 days of Activation.
This is linked to the 'No Play' condition: any outgoing call or internet usage in the calendar month for minimum 93% of GA.
Regional Demarcation Rule
Applied selectively across SRFs:
[TABLE]
| SRF | Demarcation Rule Applied |
| Cluster Sep & Oct 2025 | No — not mentioned |
| Region Nov 2025 onwards | Yes — Lower MOP cross-border GA eliminated from calculation |
| Jan 2026 Cluster & above | Yes — Regional demarcation must be considered; lower MOP cross-border GA excluded |
| ZM/TO/MDO tiers | Yes — lower MOP GA excluded as per shared file from B2C |
[/TABLE]
Lower MOP GA Exclusion
In all SRFs from Nov 2025 onwards, Lower MOP (Market Operating Price) GA is excluded from achievement based on a report shared by B2C. This applies uniformly across Cluster, Region, MDO and ZM/TO tiers.
ETSAF (ETisalat SAF) Quality Condition
GA quality is also subject to ETSAF vetting:
98% of ETSAF (against total GA before disconnection) must be vetted and accepted by Banglalink within the 7th of the following month.
Sep 2025 SRFs: SAF is a checked condition (active).
Oct 2025 onwards: SAF field unchecked in General Conditions table — superseded by the new quality GA metric.
5. KPI-03 — GA Productivity
Definition
GA Productivity measures the quality and efficiency of GA achievement — not just volume. It tracks whether the activated SIMs generate meaningful usage. This KPI appears from Nov 2025 onwards.
Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |
| Report source | GA Productivity report is shared by B2C team — not computed by IT SC |
| Usage threshold | At least 95% of total GA (before disconnection) must have BDT 5+ usage within 7 days of activation |
| Measurement basis | Measured against FCD (First Call Date) |
| Applicable period | Nov 2025 onwards for all Cluster, Region, MDO, ZM/TO SRFs |
| Bonus Point Impact | In Q4'25, GA Productivity contributes bonus points based on % of profitable base achieved |
[/TABLE]
GA Productivity Bonus Points (Q4 2025 Quarterly SRF)
For the Q4'25 quarterly SRF, a bonus/penalty point system was applied based on GA productivity:
[TABLE]
| GA Profitable Base Achievement | Points Adjustment |
| <= 75% of profitable base | -2 Points (Penalty) |
| <= 80% of profitable base | -1 Point (Penalty) |
| >= 85% of profitable base | +2 Points (Bonus) |
| >= 90% of profitable base | +3 Points (Bonus) |
| >= 95% of profitable base | +5 Points (Bonus) |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| This bonus/penalty system appears ONLY in the Q4'25 quarterly SRFs (Cluster, Region, ZM/TO). Monthly SRFs do not use this model. |
[/TABLE]
6. KPI-04 — BSO (Base Seller Objective)
Definition
BSO (Base Seller Objective) tracks the number of active base-level sellers. The exact BSO definition is referenced in the SRF but the detailed threshold is shared separately by B2C in target files.
BSO Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |
| Achievement exclusions | DRC & monobrand excluded from BSO achievement |
| Data source | IT SC + IT BI + DWH; VLR shared by B2C |
| Eligibility gate (Jan 2026) | BSO must achieve 100% of Slab 1 Target (Point Eligibility Gate) |
| Non-GA Market rule (Jan 26) | For Non-GA markets: BSO target weight = 20% (vs. 10% in GA markets) |
| Gate trigger | Used as a Point Eligibility Gate alongside LSO and SSO from Jan 2026 |
| Scope | Applied across all recipient tiers: Cluster, Region, MDO, ZM/TO |
[/TABLE]
BSO Weight in Non-GA Markets
From Jan 2026, Non-GA markets (specifically CHICOX09 & CHICOX11) have a higher BSO weighting:
[TABLE]
| Market Type | BSO Weight | LSO Weight | SSO Weight |
| GA Market | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| Non-GA Market | 20% | 20% | N/A |
[/TABLE]
7. KPI-05 — LSO (Loyalty Seller Objective)
Definition
LSO (Loyalty Seller Objective) tracks sellers maintaining loyalty/active engagement. Like BSO, the detailed threshold is defined in the B2C-provided target files.
LSO Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |
| Achievement exclusions | DRC & monobrand excluded |
| Active LSO definition | Specified in individual SRF appendix (BSO/LSO definition section) |
| Eligibility gate (Jan 2026) | LSO must achieve 100% of Slab 1 Target (Point Eligibility Gate) |
| Non-GA Market rule (Jan 26) | For Non-GA markets: LSO target weight = 20% (vs. 10% in GA markets) |
| Data source | IT SC + IT BI + DWH |
| Scope | Present in all 32 SRFs across all periods |
[/TABLE]
LSO as Point Eligibility Gate (Jan 2026 onwards)
From January 2026, LSO achievement is required to qualify for points:
LSO must reach 100% of Slab 1 Target to qualify for the Point Eligibility Gate.
In Non-GA markets the LSO gate remains at 100% of Slab 1 Target but the weighting in scoring doubles (20%).
8. KPI-06 — SSO (Secondary Seller Objective)
Definition
SSO (Secondary Seller Objective) was introduced as a new KPI from January 2026. It measures secondary-level seller performance and is used both as a scoring KPI and as a Point Eligibility Gate.
SSO Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |

| Eligibility gate | SSO must achieve 100% of Slab 1 Target (Point Eligibility Gate) |
| Scoring weight (GA mkt) | 10% of total quality execution score |
| Non-GA market | SSO not applicable in Non-GA markets (CHICOX09 & CHICOX11) |
| Data source | IT SC + IT BI + DWH |
| Scope | Jan 2026 Cluster, Region, ZM/TO/MDO SRFs |
[/TABLE]
Quality Execution Targets (Jan 2026)
The Jan 2026 SRF specifies exact quality execution thresholds:
[TABLE]
| Market | SSO Gate | BSO Gate | LSO Gate |
| GA Market | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| Non-GA Market | N/A | 20% | 20% |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]

9. KPI-07 — Total Bundle (Data + Voice + Mix)
Definition
Total Bundle is the summation of three bundle types sold by retailers to subscribers (not SC/primary lifting):
Data Pack Upsell (i-TOP UP based recharge)
Voice Bundle Upsell (i-TOP UP based recharge)
Mix Bundle Upsell (i-TOP UP based recharge)
Bundle Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Rule |
| Transaction scope | Bundle upsell means retailer to customer — SC excluded |
| DRC exclusion | DRC achievements excluded from bundle achievement in all SRFs |
| Deno list | Denomination list for valid bundles shared by B2C |
| Measurement basis | Valid recharge = i-TOP UP only (not SC) |
| Achievement period | Sep and Oct 2025 SRFs only — phased out from Nov 2025 |
| Retail SCR & VLR | Retail SCR (Sim Card Reload) & VLR achievement shared by B2C |
| Data source | IT SC + IT BI + DWH |
[/TABLE]
Bundle Phase-Out
Total Bundle as a KPI appears only in Sep and Oct 2025 SRFs. From November 2025, the campaign replaced Bundle with GA and GA Productivity as primary KPIs.
[TABLE]
| Period | Bundle KPI Status |
| Sep 2025 | Active — 1 of 4 KPIs (BSO + LSO + Recharge + Bundle) |
| Oct 2025 | Active — 1 of 4 KPIs (BSO + LSO + Recharge + Bundle) |
| Nov 2025+ | Removed — replaced by GA + GA Productivity |
[/TABLE]
10. Scoring & Point System
BCL uses a points-based ranking system. Each KPI contributes points, and the total determines the winner. The system evolved from Sep 2025 to Mar 2026.
Monthly Point System (Nov 2025 – Mar 2026)
Each KPI is scored and points are aggregated. The exact point allocation per KPI is defined in the B2C-provided target files (Detail Format). The SRFs reference 'Details format shared in the target file' without specifying exact weights.
Quarterly Point System — Q4 2025
The Q4'25 quarterly SRFs include an explicit Total Point Calculation with Bonus Point logic:
[TABLE]
| Component | Rule |
| Total Points | Sum of monthly points across Oct, Nov, Dec 2025 for each KPI |
| Bonus Point Trigger | GA Productivity vs. Profitable Base — adds or deducts points |
| Bonus: <=75% base | -2 points penalty |
| Bonus: <=80% base | -1 point penalty |
| Bonus: >=85% base | +2 points bonus |
| Bonus: >=90% base | +3 points bonus |
| Bonus: >=95% base | +5 points bonus |
| Winner criteria | Highest total points (including bonus) = Best Cluster / Region |
[/TABLE]
Quarterly Point System — Q1 2026
Q1'26 introduces a two-tier target achiever ranking separate from best points:
[TABLE]
| Category | Recharge Gate | GA Gate | Award |
| Best (Top 1 National) | — | — | Highest Total Points |
| 95% Achiever | 95% of Q1'26 Slab 1 Target | 95% of Q1'26 Slab 1 Target | National Top 1 |
| 100% Achiever | 100% of Q1'26 Slab 1 Target | 100% of Q1'26 Slab 1 Target | National Top 1 |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| ZM/MDO Q1'26: Top 3 per Cluster + National Top 5 100% Target Achievers. Post Gate Averaging is applied after gate evaluation. |
[/TABLE]
Quarterly Point System — Q4 2025 (Target Achiever)
[TABLE]
| Category | Recharge Threshold | GA Threshold | Award |
| Target Achiever | 100% of Q4'25 Target | 90% of Q4'25 Target | Best Cluster + Target Achieving Cluster |
[/TABLE]
11. Eligibility Gates
BCL SRFs use two types of gates: Campaign Eligibility Gates (to participate in ranking) and Point Eligibility Gates (to earn points). Gates are mandatory — failure to meet a gate disqualifies the participant from the relevant award.
Campaign Eligibility Gates — Evolution by Period
[TABLE]
| Period | Gate Conditions |
| Sep 2025 | Campaign Eligibility & Point Eligibility Gate to be considered (details in VLR shared by B2C) |
| Oct 2025 | Campaign Eligibility & Point Eligibility Gate to be considered (Retail SCR & VLR from B2C) |
| Nov 2025 | Campaign Eligibility & Point Eligibility Gate to be considered (VLR from B2C) |
| Dec 2025 | Campaign Eligibility & Point Eligibility Gate to be considered |
| Jan 2026 | Campaign Gate: Recharge 100% of Slab 1 Target AND GA of Slab 1 Target |
| Feb 2026 | Campaign Gate: Recharge 100% & GA 95% of Slab 1 Target |
| Mar 2026 | Campaign Gate: Recharge 100% & GA 95% of Slab 1 Target |
| Q4'25 Quarterly | Campaign Gate + Target Achiever gate (Recharge 100%, GA 90% of Q4'25 Target) |
| Q1'26 Quarterly | 95% gate (Recharge & GA 95% of Slab 1) AND 100% gate (both 100% of Slab 1) |
[/TABLE]
Point Eligibility Gates (Jan 2026 onwards)
[TABLE]
| Gate KPI | Threshold | Notes |
| BSO | 100% of Slab 1 Target | Required to earn BSO points |
| LSO | 100% of Slab 1 Target | Required to earn LSO points |
| SSO | 100% of Slab 1 Target | Required to earn SSO points; N/A in Non-GA markets |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| Jan 2026 SRF specifies explicit quality execution targets: SSO 10%, BSO 10%, LSO 10% for GA markets. Non-GA markets: BSO 20%, LSO 20%, no SSO. |
[/TABLE]

14. Exclusion Conditions
The following exclusions apply universally across all 32 BCL SRFs unless otherwise stated.
[TABLE]

BL Franchise Store Exclusion — Detail
Retailers registered in the DMS (Dealer Management System) retail database with 'BL Franchise Store' in the Retailer Type column/page are excluded from commission calculations. This condition appears verbatim in all 32 SRFs.
