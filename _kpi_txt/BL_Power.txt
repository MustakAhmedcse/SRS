## 2. Category A — Denomination (Deno) Recharge Drive
6 SRFs | Nov 2025 – Apr 2026 | Transaction Type: RECHARGE
Commission paid per successful denomination (deno) hit. Each deno has a specific BDT commission rate, date range, and optional time window.
## 2.1 Core KPI Conditions
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Denomination Recharge Hit Commission |
| Transaction Type | RECHARGE |
| Eligibility | All existing retailers on BL Power platform |
| Qualifying Criterion | Retailer must successfully recharge the exact deno amount within the specified date AND time window |
| Time Window | Most denos have specific hours (e.g., 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM); some have full-day windows |
| Peak Window Rate | Some denos have a higher commission rate during a 2-hour 'peak' window vs. standard hours |
| Counting Method | Per-hit (each valid recharge of the deno counts independently) |
| Data Source | EV Platform |
| AIT Rule | Disburse after excluding AIT |
[/TABLE]
## 2.2 Unique Commission Structures
## 2.2.1 Standard: Single Rate for Deno (Full Day or Named Window)
A single BDT commission applies for each successful hit of a given deno within the time window.
[TABLE]
| Condition | Example |
| Date range | e.g., 1 Nov 2025 – 30 Nov 2025 |
| Time window | e.g., 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM daily |
| Deno amount | e.g., BDT 65 |
| Commission (before AIT) | e.g., BDT 5.56 per hit |
| Commission (after AIT) | e.g., BDT 3.89 per hit |
[/TABLE]
## 2.2.2 Tiered: Different Rates for Peak vs. Standard Window
For certain high-value denos, two rates apply depending on the time of the transaction:
[TABLE]
| Window Type | Hours | Commission (before AIT) | Commission (after AIT) |
| Peak window | e.g., 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM | Higher rate (e.g., BDT 8.33) | e.g., BDT 5.83 |
| Standard window | Rest of day window | Lower rate (e.g., BDT 5.56) | e.g., BDT 3.89 |
[/TABLE]

## 7. Category F — Percentage-Based Add Balance Commission
3 SRFs | Jan 2026 – Mar 2026 | Transaction Type: ADD_BALANCE | Fixed percentage rate
## 7.1 KPI Conditions
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Percentage Commission on Add Balance Amount |
| Transaction Type | ADD_BALANCE |
| Commission Rate | 1.470664% of total add balance amount (including AIT) |
| AIT Rule | Stated including AIT; disburse AFTER excluding AIT |
| Eligibility | All existing BL Power retailers |
| Counting Method | Applied to cumulative add balance within the campaign period |
| Data Source | EV Platform |
[/TABLE]
## 7.2 Monthly Campaign Instances
[TABLE]
| Month | Period | Commission Rate (incl. AIT) | Disbursement |
| January 2026 | 12 – 31 Jan 2026 | 1.470664% | After excluding AIT |
| February 2026 | 1 – 28 Feb 2026 | 1.470664% | After excluding AIT |
| March 2026 | 1 – 31 Mar 2026 | 1.470664% | After excluding AIT |
[/TABLE]
📌 Note: The identical rate across all three months indicates a standardized monthly recurring commission structure. The Jan campaign started mid-month (12 Jan), while Feb and Mar were full-month campaigns.
## 7.3 Example Calculation
[TABLE]
| Retailer adds BDT 1,000 balance | Commission (incl. AIT) = 1,000 × 1.470664% = BDT 14.71 |
| After AIT deduction (~30%) | Disbursed amount ≈ BDT 14.71 × 0.70 = BDT 10.30 |
| Note | Exact AIT rate varies; always use the CB (-AIT) figure from the SRF for disbursement |
[/TABLE]
## 8. Category G — Internal Employee Campaign
1 SRF | Feb 2026 | Recipient: BL Full-Time Employees (not retailers)
## 8.1 KPI Conditions
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Employee Recharge Competition — Daraz Voucher Reward |
| Campaign Period | 15 – 28 February 2026 |
| Recipient Type | Top 20 Banglalink full-time employees |
| Reward Type | Daraz e-commerce vouchers (GIFT — not cash commission) |
| Platform Requirement | All recharges must be through the BL Power app only |
| Min Total Recharge | BDT 2,000 total recharge to qualify |
| Self-Recharge Cap | Max BDT 1,000 may be to own pool number |

| Eligibility | BL full-time employees with BL Power app account |
[/TABLE]
📌 Note: This is the only campaign in the folder targeting internal Banglalink employees rather than external retailers. The cap on self-recharges (BDT 1,000) prevents employees from simply recharging their own number to win.
