1. Campaign Overview & Structure
The Banglalink WiFi Campaign is a monthly performance incentive programme for distribution channel stakeholders promoting Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) device installation. The campaign runs from January 2026 to April 2026 across five distinct recipient tiers, each with its own incentive model.
Unlike traditional mobile sales KPIs (Recharge, GA), the BL WiFi Campaign centres entirely on a single new-product KPI: successful FWA device installation — tracking deployment of Banglalink's home internet WiFi routers/devices.
Recipient Tiers & Incentive Models
[TABLE]

| RSO | BDT 277.7778 per FWA installation | 4 | Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr 2026 |
| Cluster | GIFT reward — Top 1 Cluster Nationally | 3 | 17–28 Feb, Mar, Apr 2026 |

2. KPI-01 — Successful FWA Device Installation
The sole primary KPI across all 17 BL WiFi Campaign SRFs is Successful FWA Device Installation. This measures the number of Fixed Wireless Access (FWA) devices successfully installed at subscriber premises.
[TABLE]
| FWA-INSTALL | Successful Installation | All 17 SRFs |
[/TABLE]
Definition
FWA = Fixed Wireless Access — Banglalink's home internet WiFi device/router.
A 'Successful' installation means the device has been physically installed and commissioned at the subscriber's premises.
Performance data for FWA installations is shared by B2C for each recipient tier.
KPI Scope Rules
[TABLE]
| Rule | Details | All SRFs? |

6. Recharge Incentive (Distributor — Feb, Mar, Apr 2026)
From February 2026, Distributors earn a second incentive tied to recharge retention of FWA subscribers. This is a unique condition that appears only in Distributor SRFs and only from Feb 2026 onwards.
Recharge Bonus Rule
[TABLE]
| Field | Details |
| Condition | Subscriber installed with FWA device must recharge in the following month |
| Incentive amount | 10% of the plan value (recharge plan amount) |
| Who earns it | Distributor only — RSO does not get recharge bonus |
| When paid | Following month after successful FWA installation and recharge |
| Data source | B2C — Distributor wise FWA installation along with Recharge data |
| Jan 2026 | NOT applicable — recharge bonus not in Jan SRF |
| Feb 2026 | Applicable — introduced in Feb 2026 |
| Mar 2026 | Applicable — continued |
| Apr 2026 | Applicable — continued |
[/TABLE]
Recharge Incentive Logic
The recharge bonus creates a two-step incentive chain for Distributors:
Step 1 — Installation: Earn BDT 166.6667 per successful FWA device installed during the campaign month.
Step 2 — Retention: Earn 10% of the subscriber's recharge plan value if that subscriber recharges in the month following installation.
This retention mechanism incentivises Distributors not just to install devices but to ensure subscribers are properly onboarded and generate active recharges.
[TABLE]
| The exact recharge plan denominations are not specified in the SRF — the B2C team provides the detailed recharge data. '10% of the plan' means 10% of whatever plan amount the subscriber recharges with. |
[/TABLE]

11. Winner Evaluation Rules
Distributor & RSO — No Ranking
For Distributor and RSO tiers, there is no competitive ranking or winner evaluation. Every eligible installation earns the fixed BDT incentive. The model is purely transactional: install = earn.
Cluster, Region, SZM/ZM/TO/MDO — Ranking-Based
For the three recognition tiers, winner evaluation is based on:
Step 1 — Eligibility check: Only participants meeting the minimum device threshold are eligible.
Step 2 — Ranking: Among eligible participants, rank by total successful FWA installations (highest to top).
Step 3 — Award: Top N (per SRF) receive the GIFT award.
[TABLE]
| Tier | Tie-Break Rule | Data Source for Ranking |
| Cluster | Not explicitly specified — B2C shares cluster-wise data | B2C — Cluster wise performance |
| Region | Not explicitly specified — B2C shares region-wise data | B2C — Region wise performance data |

[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
