## 1. Folder Overview — BP GA SRF Catalogue
The 'BP GA' folder contains 15 SRFs covering short-duration BP (Brand Promoter) Gross Addition incentive campaigns. Campaigns run in 7-to-31-day windows, with multiple drives per month from January 2026 onward. All campaigns incentivize BPs to achieve GA targets using gift vouchers or cash incentives.
[TABLE]
| Campaign Type | SRF Count | Period | Reward Type | GA Counting Method |
| BP GA Bonanza | 14 | Aug 2025 – Apr 2026 | Physical gifts / Vouchers / Cash incentive | Cluster-based (Aug25) → BP Assist Code-wise (Nov25+) |
| BP GA Booster Campaign | 1 | 21–31 Jan 2026 | Cash incentive (BDT 1,500) | BP Assist Code-wise GA |
[/TABLE]

| Manager | Md Hasibul Karim |
| Department | Channel Planning |
| Recipient Type | Field Force: BP (Brand Promoters) |

[/TABLE]

## 3. KPI Core Definition — Gross Addition (GA)
[TABLE]
| KPI Full Name | Gross Addition (GA) |
| Definition | New Prepaid subscriber activations attributed to a BP, plus successful MNP (Mobile Number Portability) transfers |
| Activation Basis | FCD (First Call Date) — the date of first call on the new SIM |
| Campaign Period | Activation must occur within the campaign's Effective Date to End Date |
| Product Scope | All Prepaid connections; successful MNP included |
| Excluded | Data SIM (MMSTDATA), EV SIM, TCG Barred connections, Self-barred connections (from Nov 2025 onward) |
| TCG Bar Exclusion | Any type of TCG Bar and self-barred connections will not be counted in achievement (explicit from Nov 2025) |
| Achievement % | Calculated as: (Actual GA ÷ Target GA) × 100, rounded per rounding rule |
| Threshold to Qualify | Varies by SRF — 100% of target required for each slab; or minimum absolute GA count |
[/TABLE]
## 4. GA Counting Method — Two Variants
Two distinct methods for attributing GA to a BP were used across the 15 SRFs:
## 4.1 Variant A: Cluster-Based GA (Aug 2025 SRF only)
The Aug 2025 SRF used a cluster-differentiated GA attribution model:
[TABLE]
| SRF | BP GA Bonanza_1st to 31st Aug25 |
| Cluster Rule 1 — North East & West Cluster | GA means FCD. BP Wise Retailer code will be shared by B2C. |
| Cluster Rule 2 — North, East & Central Cluster | BP wise designated Retailer FCD data AND BP wise SMART POS (CODE: ARRANGED-3) both GA will be considered for incentive. (BP wise Retailer code shared by B2C.) |
| Implication | In some clusters, SMART POS data supplements FCD data for GA counting — broadening what counts as a GA for BPs in those regions |
[/TABLE]
Note: This dual-source (FCD + SMART POS) GA attribution was unique to the Aug 2025 SRF and was not carried forward to later SRFs.
## 4.2 Variant B: BP Assist Code-Wise GA (Nov 2025 – Apr 2026)
[TABLE]
| SRFs | All 14 SRFs from Nov 2025 onward (including Booster Campaign) |
| Formula | BP GA = BP Assist Code wise GA |
| Meaning | GA is attributed to a BP based on their unique 'Assist Code' — connections assisted or facilitated by that BP code |
| Standardisation | Single, uniform counting method applied across all clusters and regions |
| Advantage | Eliminates cluster-specific variation; all BPs measured the same way regardless of geography |
[/TABLE]
## 5. Gate Conditions — 48hr Usage & VLR Active Thresholds
All 15 SRFs require both gate conditions to be met as a prerequisite for incentive eligibility. A BP failing either gate is disqualified regardless of GA achievement.
[TABLE]
| Gate 1 — 48hr Usage | ≥ X% of activated subscribers must have 48-hour usage (threshold varies by SRF) |
| Gate 2 — VLR Active | ≥ X% of activated subscribers must be VLR Active (threshold varies by SRF) |
| Data Provider | B2C team shares achievement data for both gates |
| Consequence of Failure | BP is ineligible for any incentive regardless of GA count |
[/TABLE]

## 5.2 Special VLR Prorated Rule — Apr 2026 Phase 2 (11–20 Apr)
The Apr 2026 Phase 2 SRF introduced a unique prorated VLR rule for the North Cluster only:
[TABLE]
| Standard Rule (all clusters) | VLR ≥85% → full incentive eligible |
| North Cluster — Full Incentive | VLR ≥85% → receives 100% of applicable incentive |
| North Cluster — Prorated Incentive | VLR ≥75% and <85% → receives prorated incentive based on VLR% achieved |
| North Cluster — No Incentive | VLR <75% → not eligible for any incentive |
| 48hr Gate | ≥85% for all clusters (no North Cluster exception for 48hr) |
| TCG/Self-Bar | Any type of TCG Bar and self-barred connections excluded from achievement |
[/TABLE]
Note: This prorated VLR gate was unique to the Apr 2026 Phase 2 SRF — the first and only instance of a partial/prorated gate condition in the entire BP GA series.
## 6. Reward / Incentive Structures
Five distinct incentive structure types were used across the 15 BP GA SRFs:
## 6.1 Structure Type 1 — Weekly/Monthly Slab with Physical Gifts (Aug 2025)
The Aug 2025 SRF used a 4-week cumulative target model with physical gift rewards:
[TABLE]
| SRF | BP GA Bonanza_1st to 31st Aug25 |
| Period | 1–31 August 2025 (full month) |
| Target Type | Cumulative weekly targets with overlapping windows |
| Minimum GA to Qualify | Not stated — slab achievement drives eligibility |
| Max Gifts | 1 gift per BP (highest slab reached) |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| Week | Date Range | Cumulative Target | Slab Achieved If... | Reward |
| Week 1 | 1–7 Aug 2025 | Target: 20 GA | (no standalone slab) | — |
| Week 2 | 1–14 Aug 2025 | Target: 40 GA | 100% Wk 2 + ≥90% monthly target | Grocery Box (BDT 500 worth) |
| Week 3 | 1–21 Aug 2025 | Target: 60 GA | 100% Wk 3 + ≥95% monthly target | Iron (BDT 800 worth) |
| Week 4 | 1–31 Aug 2025 | Target: 80 GA | 100% Week 4 target | Melamain Set (BDT 1,200 worth) |
[/TABLE]
Note: The Aug 2025 slab eligibility requires BOTH weekly AND monthly achievement simultaneously. This dual-condition gate (e.g., Slab 1 = 100% of Week 2 target AND ≥90% monthly target) was the most complex slab structure in the entire BP GA series.

Nov and Dec 2025 used a phase-based voucher structure with simpler single-target achievement:

| Phase 2 | 1–15 Nov 2025 | B2C-set target | ≥90% / ≥90% | ≥100% of Phase 2 target | BDT 500 worth voucher |
| Phase 3 | 1–22 Nov 2025 | B2C-set target | ≥90% / ≥90% | ≥100% of Phase 3 target | BDT 500 worth voucher |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]

Dec 2025 — Single Phase:

## 6.4 Structure Type 4 — Single-Target Flat Cash Incentive (Booster Campaign)
The Booster Campaign used a simpler single flat-rate incentive:
[TABLE]
| SRF | BP GA Booster Campaign_21st to 31st Jan26 |
| Period | 21–31 January 2026 |
| Target | Achieve 40 GA |
| Commission | BDT 1,500 incentive (no slabs — binary: hit 40 GA or nothing) |
| Gate | 48hr ≥80%, VLR ≥80% |
| AIT | Not specified |
| ETSAF | 98% of ETSAF (Total against activation before Disconnection) must be vetted and accepted by Banglalink within 7th of following month |
| SAF Condition | Explicit 98% ETSAF requirement stated (more detail than other SRFs) |
| Recharge Detail | Expanded definition: EV (SR to Retailer R2RT and distributor to Retailer D2RT), DTR (Manual and Daily Commission from Salescom) and OTF (EV OTF + IRIS OTF), S.C (Retail lifting per DMS entry), Data/Voice/Mix bundle (i-TOP up) |
[/TABLE]
Note: The Booster Campaign's recharge section is the most detailed in the series — explicitly listing R2RT, D2RT, DTR, OTF, IRIS OTF sub-types. This level of detail was not present in other BP GA SRFs.
## 6.5 Structure Type 5 — Cumulative Phase Makeup Incentive (Apr 2026 Phase 2)
The Apr 2026 Phase 2 SRF introduced a unique combined Phase 1+2 makeup option:
[TABLE]
| SRF | BP GA Bonanza_11th to 20th Apr26 |
| Standard Phase 2 Slabs | Slab 1: 30 GA → BDT 800 | Slab 2: 40 GA → BDT 1,600 |
| Makeup Option (Phase 1+2 Combined) | Slab 1: 60 GA cumulative → BDT 1,300 | Slab 2: 80 GA cumulative → BDT 2,600 |
| Makeup Purpose | Allows BPs who missed Phase 1 target (or only achieved Slab 1 in Phase 1) to earn higher incentive by combining Phase 1+2 GA |
| Deduction Rule | From Phase 1+2 incentive, deduct any incentive already received in Phase 1 |
| Eligibility for Makeup | Only for BPs who: (a) missed Phase 1 target entirely, OR (b) achieved only Slab 1 in Phase 1 |
| Exclusion from Makeup | Who achieved Slab 2 in Phase 1 → not eligible for Phase 1+2 Slab 1 makeup |
[/TABLE]
Note: This makeup/catch-up mechanism is the only instance in the BP GA series where performance from two consecutive periods is combined for incentive eligibility. It effectively gives struggling BPs a second chance.
## 7. ETSAF & SAF Conditions
[TABLE]
| Standard ETSAF (most SRFs) | Above ETSAF QC condition will be considered as implemented (general reference) |
| Aug 2025 Explicit ETSAF | At least 98% (Total against activation before Disconnection) ETSAF vetted/submitted and accepted within 7th of following month |
| Booster Campaign ETSAF | 98% of ETSAF (Total against activation before Disconnection) must be vetted and accepted by Banglalink within 7th of following month |
| ETSAF Scope | Applied against all activations before disconnection — not just active subscribers |
| Deadline | 7th of following month from activation date |
| Consequence | Non-compliance with ETSAF gate disqualifies the BP from incentive |
[/TABLE]
Note: Most SRFs reference ETSAF generically ('above ETSAF QC condition will be considered'). The Aug 2025 and Booster Campaign SRFs are the only ones with explicit 98% thresholds stated — all SRFs implicitly carry this standard.
