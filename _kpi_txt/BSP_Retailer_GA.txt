7. First Recharge (1st Recharge) Gate — Conditions & Window
[TABLE]
| Applied From | Aug 2025 onwards (not present in May, Jun, Jul 2025 SRFs) |
| Purpose | Quality gate — ensures new activations make a first recharge, indicating genuine usage |
| Threshold | >=95% of GA must have 1st Recharge (Aug–Feb26) | >=90% for Slab 1 only in Sep25 |
| Recharge Window | 30 days after activation date (Aug–Oct 2025) | 45 days after activation date (Feb 2026+) |
| Valid Recharge | All Valid Recharges (C2S recharges — valid bundle recharge/upsell; I-TOP up — in Weekly SRFs) |
| Effect of Gate | If 1st Recharge % falls below threshold → that agent's incentive for the slab is not paid |
| Data Source | System data for 1st Recharge tracking. 30/45 day window counted from FCD. |
[/TABLE]
Note: Sep 2025 introduced differentiated 1st Recharge thresholds per slab: Slab 1 requires only >=90% while Slabs 2 and 3 require >=95%.

9. Jun 2025 Special — Multi-Modality Campaign
The Jun 2025 SRF (effective 1–22 Jun 2025) introduced a unique multi-modality structure not seen in other BSP Retailer GA SRFs. It combines three commission modalities in a single campaign.
9.1 Modality 1 — Upfront + Conditional Weekly/Monthly Pack Commission
[TABLE]
| Structure | Per-agent upfront commission based on GA achievement % vs. target, PLUS per-activation conditional commissions for pack purchases |
| Products | BL Prepaid including RYZE Prepaid |
| Max GA Cap | 100 GA for both slabs |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| GA Ach % vs Target | Upfront Commission | Conditional 1: Weekly Pack in Jul25 | Conditional 2: Monthly Pack in Jul25 |
| 90% to 115% | BDT 1,000 / Agent | BDT 10 / Activation | BDT 20 / Activation |
| > 115% | BDT 1,500 / Agent | BDT 10 / Activation | BDT 20 / Activation |
[/TABLE]
Note: For both weekly and monthly pack purchases in July 2025, the maximum commission should be considered (i.e., take the higher of the two if both apply).
Note: Eligible pack prices listed in SRF: Weekly Packs (BDT 19, 60, 86, 96, 116, 129, 149, 156, 169, 177, 198, 199, 209, 219, 227, 239, 277, 298, 319, 358, 398, 458, 498, 558, 658, 854) and Monthly Packs (BDT 50, 86, 96, 100, 146, 156, 196, 216, 219, 296, 299, 306, 348, 377, 396, 399, 448, 449, 498, 499, 548, 549, 577, 648, 649, 677, 706, 707, 719, 729, 748, 777, 848, 849, 859, 898, 977, 998, 1199, 1258, 1299, 1599) and others.
9.2 Modality 2 — Daily Participation in GA
[TABLE]
| Commission | BDT 500 / CCR |
| Condition | >=100% — number of unique GA activation days vs. scheduled working days in the month |
| Rule | Count of unique calendar days with at least one activation must equal or exceed all scheduled working days |
[/TABLE]
9.3 App Installation Requirements (Jun 2025)
[TABLE]
| RYZE App | MUST be installed for New RYZE Prepaid GA. Valid if any RYZE App activity found in source from activation date to any date. |
| MyBL App | MUST be installed for new BL Prepaid GA for smartphone users. Valid if any MyBL App activity found in source from activation date to any date. |
[/TABLE]
Note: Maturity date for Jun 2025 SRF: Last day of following month (July 31, 2025) — to allow time for pack purchase tracking in July.

11.1 48-Hour Usage Gate — VLR Payout Tiers
[TABLE]
| Gate Name | 48-Hour Usage Gate |
| Rule | New activations must show valid usage within 48 hours of activation |
| Data Source | 48-hour data taken from system |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| % SIM with 48hr Usage | Payout Rule |
| >=95% of SIM | Full incentive payment |
| 80% to <95% of SIM | Prorated incentive — only zero-usage SIM incentive deducted |
| <80% of SIM | No incentive payment — complete forfeiture |
[/TABLE]
Note: Prorated rule: for 80–94.99% usage compliance, only the incentive for SIMs with zero usage is deducted. SIMs that DO have usage still get full commission.

11.3 Weekly Cumulative Deduction Mechanism
Each week's SRF covers the FULL month up to that date, and deducts the prior week's commission amount to avoid double payment. This creates a cumulative progressive disbursement:
[TABLE]
| SRF | Coverage Period | Deduction from Prior SRF | Agent List Date |
| Mar Week 1 | 1–7 Mar | None (first SRF) | 7th Mar 2026 |
| Mar Week 2 | 1–21 Mar | Deducts 1st–7th Mar amount | 21st Mar 2026 |
| Mar Week 3 | 1–31 Mar | Deducts 1st–21st Mar amount | 31st Mar 2026 |
| Apr Week 1 | 1–7 Apr | None (first Apr SRF) | 7th Apr 2026 |
| Apr Week 2 | 1–14 Apr | Deducts 1st–7th Apr amount | 14th Apr 2026 |
| Apr Week 3 | 1–21 Apr | Deducts 1st–14th Apr amount | 21st Apr 2026 |
| Apr Week 4 | 1–30 Apr | Deducts 1st–21st Apr amount | 30th Apr 2026 |
[/TABLE]
Note: Agent list used for eligibility corresponds to the END date of the coverage period for each SRF (snapshot of active agents on that date).

15. AIT & Rounding Rules
[TABLE]
| AIT — Type 1 (RYZE) | NOT applicable — explicitly stated: "AIT will not be applicable for this commission (This is applicable for Sales Team)" |
