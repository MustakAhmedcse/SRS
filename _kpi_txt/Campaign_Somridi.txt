5. Recharge KPI — Definition, Components & Cluster Modalities
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Recharge (EV Secondary + SC Primary) |
| EV Secondary Definition | RSO to Retailer (R2RT) + Distributor to Retailer (D2RT) i-top-up sales + Manual and Daily Commission from Salescom + EV OTF + IRIS OTF |
| SC Primary Definition | Scratch Card sales = SCs lifted from Warehouse to DD (primary lifting). S.C means Retail lifting as per DMS entry. |
| Recharge Formula | EV Secondary + SC Primary, LESS Withdrawal (taken as usual process) |
| C2C Inclusion | C2C from Digital Distribution Retailers is included. Retailer list shared by B2C. |
| DRC Exclusion | DRC EV Secondary excluded from achievement. B2C shares DRC list. |
| Employee Pool Exclusion | Any recharge at BL employee pool phone number NOT considered (from Oct 2025 onwards) |
| Withdrawal | Less withdrawal applied — as usual process |
[/TABLE]

14. Individual KPI Assessment — C2C & SC Evaluation Rule
When a DH fails to meet the minimum Recharge KPI threshold, C2C and SC are evaluated individually (instead of cumulatively). The DH is paid the higher of: (a) Cumulative EV+SC assessment, or (b) Individual KPI assessment.
14.1 Threshold Rules by Version
[TABLE]
| SRF | Recharge Min Threshold | C2C Weight (standard DH) | C2C Weight (CHICOX09/11) | SC Weight | Other Parameter Min |
| Gold Sep 2025 | 95% | 30% | 40% | 10% | 75% of other parameter |
| Gold Oct 2025 | 70% | 30% | 40% | 10% | 80% of other parameter |
[/TABLE]
14.2 Individual KPI Logic (Example)
[TABLE]
| Scenario | C2C Ach | SC Ach | Result | Reason |
| C2C passes, SC passes (Sep25) | 100% | >=85% | 100% C2C KPI payout | C2C 100% + SC >=85% (75% min met) |
| C2C passes, SC fails (Sep25) | 100% | <85% | Not eligible | SC below 75% minimum of other parameter |
| SC passes, C2C passes (Sep25) | >=85% | 100% | 100% SC KPI payout | SC 100% + C2C >=85% (75% min met) |
| SC passes, C2C fails (Sep25) | <85% | 100% | Not eligible | C2C below 75% minimum of other parameter |
| C2C passes, SC passes (Oct25) | 100% | >=80% | 100% C2C KPI payout | C2C 100% + SC >=80% (80% min met) |
| C2C passes, SC fails (Oct25) | 100% | <80% | Not eligible | SC below 80% minimum |
[/TABLE]
Note: Both EV & SC are assessed separately AND cumulatively for every DH. The DH receives whichever assessment gives the higher payout amount.

18. Growth-Based KPI (Mar 2026)
[TABLE]
| Introduced In | Distributor Campaign Somridi Mar26 (new feature vs prior months) |
| Concept | Month-over-month GA growth rate, normalised for different day counts between Last Month (LM) and Current Month (CM) |
| Formula | Growth % = ((CM GA / CM Days) - (LM GA / LM Days)) / (LM GA / LM Days) × 100 |
| Purpose | Compares daily average GA across months with unequal day counts (e.g., Feb=28 days vs Mar=31 days) |
[/TABLE]
18.1 Growth Calculation Example (from SRF)
[TABLE]
| LM Day Count | CM Day Count | LM GA | CM GA | Growth % |
| 28 | 31 | 100 | 102 | -8%  (102/31 vs 100/28 — lower daily avg) |
| 28 | 31 | 120 | 121 | -9%  (121/31 vs 120/28 — lower daily avg) |
| 28 | 31 | 115 | 135 | 6%  (135/31 vs 115/28 — higher daily avg) |
[/TABLE]
Note: The growth calculation normalises absolute GA counts by dividing by the number of days in each month before comparing, ensuring fair comparison between months of different lengths.
19. Universal / General Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Value / Rule |
| Activation Date | FCD (First Call Date) — activation during the promotion period (calendar month) |
| ETSAF | 98% of ETSAF (total against activation before disconnection) must be vetted and accepted by Banglalink within 7th of following month |
| Lifting/SC Primary | Primary SC = DD lifting. SC sales = scratch cards lifted from Warehouse to DD. |
| Valid Recharge (EV) | EV Secondary: R2RT + D2RT i-top-up + Manual & Daily Commission from Salescom + EV OTF + IRIS OTF. Less withdrawal. |
| Valid Recharge (SC) | SC Primary: Retail lifting as per DMS entry. |
| DRC Exclusion | DRC EV excluded. B2C shares DRC list. |
| Employee Pool Exclusion | BL employee pool phone number recharges excluded (from Oct 2025 onwards) |
| Usage / No Play | Any outgoing call or internet usage in calendar month in minimum 93% of GA |
| Disconnection | Between FCD and latest data up to report generation date (T-6) |
| Product Scope (Sep–Nov) | i-top-up, SC, Data Upsell, GA, Mix Bundle, Voice Bundle upsell |
| Product Scope (Feb–Mar 26) | i-top-up, SC, GA, LSO (narrower — Mix Bundle and Voice Bundle upsell removed from explicit listing) |
| RDB | Month end RDB used for settlement — Recharge Data Base snapshot at month end |
| Rounding | General mathematical rounding: <0.5 rounds down, >=0.5 rounds up — applies to all KPI achievement calculations |
| Data Sources | IT SC, IT BI, DWH, POS (all SRFs) |
| Maturity Date | 8th of the following month (all 14 SRFs) |
| Target Sharing | B2C shares DD-wise targets in separate Excel files prior to calculation |
[/TABLE]
