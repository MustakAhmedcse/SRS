3. Campaign Structure — Decadal (10-day) Cycle
Campaign Win Together runs on a decadal cycle — each SRF covers a ~10-day period within a calendar month. This allows for mid-month incentive adjustments and targeted cluster-specific campaigns within the same period.
[TABLE]
| Period | SRF(s) | Notes |
| 1–10 Jan 2026 | 2 SRFs: (a) All clusters — C2S; (b) West+East only — C2C | Two parallel SRFs for same dates but different clusters and different KPI type (C2S vs C2C) |
| 11–20 Jan 2026 | 1 SRF — All clusters — C2C | Standard decadal. 120% cap introduced. |
| 21–30 Jan 2026 | 1 SRF — All clusters — C2C | Standard decadal. |
| 1–10 Feb 2026 | 1 SRF — All clusters — C2C | February cycle. Same structure. Note: Maturity date is 31st Jan (retroactive/cutoff). |
[/TABLE]
Note: The 1st–10th Jan 2026 period has TWO simultaneous SRFs: one for all clusters using C2S metric, and one for West & East cluster only using C2C metric. Both cover the same dates but are processed as separate reports.
4. KPI 1: C2S / C2C Volume — Definition & Difference
[TABLE]
| Metric | Full Form | Direction | Definition | SRF(s) Using It |
| C2S | Customer to Subscriber | Recharge goes from DD/RSO → Retailer → Customer (end user) | Any amount recharged to a customer (end subscriber). Includes valid bundle recharge upsell. Valid recharge = I-TOP up. | 1st–10th Jan (all cluster SRF) |
| C2C | Customer to Customer (trade channel) | Transfer from DD/RSO → Retailer (trade-level) | Any amount transferred to a Retailer from DD or RSO. Measured as trade-level recharge flow — DD/RSO crediting retailer accounts. | West+East Jan, 11–20 Jan, 21–30 Jan, 1–10 Feb |
[/TABLE]
Note: Despite the name "Customer to Customer", C2C in this context refers to the channel transfer — DD/RSO pushing recharge credit to retailer accounts. C2S refers to the downstream flow where recharge reaches the end customer.
Note: From 11 Jan 2026 onwards, all SRFs (including all-cluster) switched to C2C. The initial all-cluster SRF (1–10 Jan) uniquely used C2S.

5. KPI 2: Winning Retail — Definition & Measurement
[TABLE]
| Definition | Number of retailers from the selective list who individually achieve their C2S/C2C target — called "Winning Retailers" |
| Target | DD must achieve both: (a) Total C2S/C2C volume target AND (b) Winning Retailer count target |
| Winning Retailer | A retailer who achieves its individual C2S/C2C target within the selective list |
| Measurement | Winning Retail Count vs. Total Retail Count from selective list = % Winning Retail Achievement |
| Data Source | Retailer list provided by B2C. Retail-wise C2S/C2C targets also shared by B2C. |
| Payout | Separate slab-based payout for Winning Retail KPI — independent of C2S/C2C Volume payout |
| Max Cap | 120% payout cap applies to Winning Retail KPI (from 11 Jan 2026 onwards) |
[/TABLE]
Note: Both KPIs (C2S/C2C Volume AND Winning Retail) must be achieved for the DD to earn incentive. Each KPI has its own independent achievement % and slab-based payout.
6. Slab-Based Payout Structure & Max Cap
Both KPI 1 (C2S/C2C Volume) and KPI 2 (Winning Retail) use slab-based payout structures. The specific slab thresholds and BDT amounts per slab are shared by B2C in a distributor-wise incentive file — they are not printed directly in the SRF text (they appear as a table/chart in the PDF which is not text-extractable). The rules governing the slabs are:
[TABLE]
| Slab Basis | % Achievement vs. Target for each KPI independently |
| Max Payout Cap | 120% — DD can earn maximum 120% payout in BOTH KPIs (applied from 11 Jan 2026 onwards) |
| 1st–10th Jan SRF Cap | Not explicitly stated — max cap rule introduced from 11 Jan SRF onwards |
| Incentive Amount Basis | Distributor-wise total campaign incentive amount shared by B2C — the % slab determines what fraction of that total is earned |
| KPI Independence | Volume KPI and Winning Retail KPI are assessed independently — each has its own slab and payout |
| Rounding | General mathematical rounding: <0.5 rounds down, >=0.5 rounds up — applies to all achievement calculations |
[/TABLE]

7. Selective Retail List — Target Assignment Mechanism
[TABLE]
| List Source | B2C provides a selective/curated list of retailers per DD — not all retailers in the DD's territory |
| Scope Restriction | DD must achieve C2S/C2C volume TARGET specifically from/to THESE selected retailers only |
| Winning Retail Target | Based on the count of retailers from this selective list who individually hit their target |
| List Update | Agent list (for eligibility) is determined as of the END DATE of each campaign period (e.g., 10th Jan, 20th Jan, 30th Jan, etc.) |
| Target Sharing | DD-wise Selective Retail List and C2S/C2C Target shared by B2C before campaign start |
| Campaign Incentive | Distributor-wise Total Campaign Incentive amount also shared by B2C |
[/TABLE]
Note: The selective retail list approach means not all retailers under a DD are included in the campaign. Only the pre-approved list matters for both Volume and Winning Retail KPIs.

[TABLE]

| Contrast | Unlike BSP CCR KPI SRFs which specify "98% ETSAF by 7th of following month", this SRF defers to whatever ETSAF QC condition is currently implemented |

12. Agent List Snapshot Policy
[TABLE]
| Rule | The agent list (DD eligibility) is taken as a snapshot of the end date of each campaign period |
| Purpose | Only DDs who are active/listed on the last day of the period are eligible for that period's incentive |
| Snapshot Dates | 1–10 Jan: 10th Jan | 11–20 Jan: 20th Jan | 21–30 Jan: 30th Jan | 1–10 Feb: 10th Feb |
| Retail List | Retailer eligibility list is also fixed at the campaign end date (via B2C selective list) |
| New DDs | DDs who join or are assigned after the period end date are not eligible for that period |
[/TABLE]
