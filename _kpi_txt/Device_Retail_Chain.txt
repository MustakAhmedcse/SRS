2. SRF Version Timeline & Structural Changes
[TABLE]

3. KPI Framework — May 2025 (Baseline)
5-KPI structure with simple pro-rata payout. Maturity: 8th of following month.
[TABLE]
| KPI | Weight | Threshold | Payout Rule | Product Scope |
| GA (Gross Add) | 40% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | Prepaid incl. RYZE + MNP |
| Recharge & Bundle | 20% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | Overall recharge; ≥40% must be data/mix bundle |
| Daily Participation | 20% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | Unique days with GA / scheduled working days |
| App Install | 10% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | App installations attributed to SSE |
| Attendance | 10% | 100% | 100% attendance = full payout; any absence = 0% | Manual data from business team; binary gate |
[/TABLE]
Note: Total weight = 100%. Below threshold for any KPI = 0% for that KPI component. Payout = sum of individual KPI payouts × BDT 4,000.

Identical to May 2025 in all conditions. Only change: GA product scope excludes RYZE.
[TABLE]

| Recharge & Bundle | 20% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | Overall recharge; ≥40% must be data/mix bundle |

| App Install | 10% | 80% | ≥80% to <100%: payout at actual %; ≥100%: 100% payout | App installations attributed to SSE |
| Attendance | 10% | 100% | 100% attendance = full payout; any absence = 0% | Manual data from business team; binary gate |

Note: RYZE exclusion is the sole difference from May 2025. All weights, thresholds, payout formula, and maturity (8th of next month) remain identical.

8. Payout Structure Evolution
8.1 May & July 2025 — Simple Pro-Rata
For each KPI: if achievement ≥ threshold → payout = actual achievement % × KPI weight × BDT 4,000. No outperformance bonus.
[TABLE]
| Achievement vs Target | Payout |
| Below threshold (e.g., <80%) | 0% |
| At or above threshold, below 100% | Actual achievement % (pro-rata) |
| At or above 100% | 100% (capped) |
[/TABLE]
8.2 October 2025 — Tiered Slabs (No Outperformance)
Each slab band pays a fixed % regardless of exact achievement within the band. Capped at 100%.
[TABLE]
| Band | Payout % | Applies To |
| <80% | 0% | GA Target, Recharge, App Install |
| 80–84.99% | 60% | GA Target, Recharge, App Install |
| 85–89.99% | 70% | GA Target, Recharge, App Install |
| 90–94.99% | 80% | GA Target, Recharge, App Install |
| 95–99.99% | 90% | GA Target, Recharge, App Install |
| ≥100% | 100% | All KPIs |
| <90% | 0% | Daily Participation |
| 90–94.99% | 60% | Daily Participation |
| 95–99.99% | 70% | Daily Participation |
[/TABLE]
8.3 January 2026 — Tiered Slabs with Outperformance Bonus
New: exceeding 100% target can yield 110% or 120% payout (overachievement bands introduced).
[TABLE]
| KPI | Floor Threshold | 95% Band | 110% Band | 120% Band |
| GA Target Ach | 93% | 93–101% | 101–108% | ≥108% |
| Recharge & Bundle | 92% | 92–98% | 98–103% | ≥103% |
| FWA Home Internet | 93% | 93–101% | 101–108% | ≥108% |
| Daily Participation | 95% | 95–100% | ≥100% → 110% | N/A |
| App Install | 81.5% | 81.5–87.2% | 87.2–91.6% | ≥91.6% → 120% |
[/TABLE]
Note: Outperformance bands mean total payout can exceed BDT 4,000 base if multiple KPIs achieve 110–120% bands.
9. Maturity & Payment Conditions
[TABLE]
| Version | Maturity Date | Condition |
| May 2025 | 8th of following month | Fixed calendar date. All KPIs lock on month-end; pay on 8th. |
| July 2025 | 8th of following month | Same as May 2025. |
| October 2025 | 45 days after activation | Maturity linked to last SIM activation in the month. GA First Recharge also uses 45-day rolling window. Delays payout vs. previous versions. |

[/TABLE]
Note: The shift from 8th-of-month to 45-days-after-activation (Oct 2025 onwards) significantly changes cash flow timing for SSEs with late-month activations.
10. Key Definitions & Glossary
[TABLE]
| Term | Definition |
| SSE | Sales Support Executive — the field force role under Device Retail Chain vertical |
| GA (Gross Add) | Successful new SIM activation. Counted per activation date. Scope varies by SRF version. |
| MNP | Mobile Number Portability — porting from another operator counts as a GA |
| RYZE | Banglalink's RYZE-branded product. Included in GA scope May 2025, excluded from July 2025 onwards. |
| FWA | Fixed Wireless Access — broadband home internet delivered via mobile network. New KPI in Jan 2026. |
| First Recharge | The first recharge transaction by a newly activated subscriber. Measured within 45 days of activation. |
| Daily Participation | Number of unique days SSE recorded GA or recharge activity ÷ scheduled working days in month. |
| App Install | Mobile app installation attributed to the SSE. Target set at 50% of GA target (Oct 2025 onwards). |
| Attendance | Physical/system-registered attendance. Binary gate — 100% required. Source: manual (May/Jul) or system (Oct/Jan). |
| Pro-Rata Payout | Payout equals actual achievement %. If GA is 85%, payout for that KPI = 85% × weight × base. |
| Tiered Slab | Fixed payout % for a range of achievement. E.g., 80–85% range pays 60% regardless of exact value. |
| Outperformance Band | Payout above 100% base (e.g., 110%, 120%) for exceeding 100% target. Introduced Jan 2026. |
| Bundle Condition | ≥40% of total recharge must come from data or mixed bundles (consistent across all versions). |
| Maturity | The date on which a KPI result is locked and payment is processed. |
| 45-Day Window | Rolling period from SIM activation during which first recharge or FWA install must occur to count. |
| Threshold | Minimum achievement level to earn any payout for a KPI. Below = 0% for that component. |
[/TABLE]
