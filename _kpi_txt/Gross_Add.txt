Day-Count-Adjusted Growth Calculation (unique condition):
Growth between months is adjusted for day-count difference. Example: LM had 28 days, CM has 31 days. If LM recharge=100 and CM=102, adjusted growth = (102/31x28)/100 - 1 = -8% (NOT +2%). This prevents apparent growth from simply having more days.
