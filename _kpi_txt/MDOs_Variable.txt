2. KPI Set Evolution by Epoch (Apr25 → Mar26)
The KPI set for MDOs Variable Incentive evolved significantly over the 12-month period. Four distinct epochs are identifiable based on structural changes to which KPIs are active and how they are defined.
[TABLE]
| Epoch | Period | Active KPIs | Key Changes |
| 1 | Apr25 – Jun25 | EV Secondary (Recharge) + SC Primary + Total Bundles + Active LSO + BSO | Baseline set. No GA, no Site Monetization. Maturity 1st–3rd of next month. |
| 2 | Jul25 – Sep25 | EV Secondary + SC Primary + Total Bundles + Active LSO + BSO + GA + Site Monetization (Revenue Bucket) | GA and Site Monetization added. Monobrand excluded. CHICOX09 prorated condition added. Maturity 10th. |
| 3 | Oct25 – Dec25 | EV Secondary + SC Primary + Active LSO + BSO + GA + Site Monetization (Absolute LUS Conversion) | Site Monetization redefined: absolute LUS→Non-LUS conversion (threshold: Core >=200K, Non-Core >=150K). Nov25 adds CHICOX09 weightage table. DHKGAZ08/MYMTAN18 RSO-wise rule added in Nov25. |
| 4 | Jan26 – Mar26 | EV Secondary + SC Primary + Active LSO + SSO + GA + Site Monetization (Absolute LUS Conversion) | BSO replaced by SSO as quality KPI. Active LSO raised: 7 days + BDT 500 C2S. VLR condition added to GA. Usage gate changed to 93% No-Play. CHICOX09 weightage restructured. |
[/TABLE]
Note: Bundle KPI (Total Bundles) was present in Epoch 1 & 2. It disappears from the main KPI list in Nov25 onward (Epoch 3 & 4), though bundle recharge remains defined in the recharge section.
3. KPI Definitions — Active LSO
The Active LSO definition changed significantly between Epochs 1–3 and Epoch 4. The threshold for both days and C2S value was raised from Jan26 onward, and the Core/Non-Core split was removed.
[TABLE]
| Epoch | Period | Active LSO Definition | Change? |
| 1, 2, 3 | Apr25 – Dec25 | Core: C2S (Retail to sub) by Retail in min 4 days of a month + BDT 200 C2S Value in total in a month Non-Core: C2S by Retail in min 4 days of a month + BDT 100 C2S Value in total in a month | Baseline |
| 4 | Jan26 – Mar26 | C2S (Retail to sub) by Retail in min 7 days of a month + BDT 500 C2S Value in total in a month (No Core/Non-Core split — single unified threshold) | CHANGED: days raised 4→7; C2S value raised 200/100→500; Core/Non-Core split removed |
[/TABLE]
Note: The Jan26 change represents a meaningful tightening of the LSO quality standard — retailers now need to transact on 7 days (not 4) with a higher C2S value. DRC exclusion applied to LSO from Jul25 onward.

6. KPI Definitions — Site Monetization: Phase 1 (Revenue Bucket Movement, Jul–Sep25)
Site Monetization was introduced in Jul25 as a 'Revenue Bucket Movement' KPI. It measures growth in service revenue at selected BTS sites above a fixed base month (May 2025).
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Site Monetization (LUS Conversion — Revenue Bucket Movement) |
| Active Period | Jul25, Aug25, Sep25 |
| Revenue Metric | Month-end Service Revenue at selected BTS |
| Base Revenue | May'25 actual Service Revenue (fixed base for Jul, Aug, Sep 25) |
| Growth Target | Service Revenue Achievement minus Base Revenue |
| Achievement Scope | Calculated based on selected BTS only |
| Payout | Prorated payout from 60%–100% achievement (example: 60% ach → 60% of BDT 1,000; 100% ach → BDT 1,000; >100% capped at BDT 1,000) |
| Maturity | 9th of the following month |
| Example (Jul25) | Total BTS=5, Service Rev Target=100, Base=90, Growth Target=10, Actual=96 (96%), Growth Ach=6 (60%), Payout=60%, Amount=BDT 600 |
[/TABLE]
7. KPI Definitions — Site Monetization: Phase 2 (Absolute LUS Conversion, Oct25 onward)
From Oct25 onward, Site Monetization was fundamentally redefined. It no longer tracks revenue bucket movement but instead measures the absolute conversion of LUS (Low Usage Sites) to Non-LUS status based on a fixed service revenue threshold.
[TABLE]
| KPI Name | Site Monetization (LUS Conversion — Absolute) |
| Active Period | Oct25, Nov25, Dec25, Jan26, Feb26, Mar26 |
| LUS Conversion Threshold — Core | Service Revenue >= BDT 200,000 per month |
| LUS Conversion Threshold — Non-Core | Service Revenue >= BDT 150,000 per month |
| Revenue Metric | Month-end Service Revenue |
| BTS Scope | Selected BTS only; BTS-wise DH mapping used; MDO availability considered |
| LUS Reference List | Oct'25 LUS list shared by B2C |
| Payout Basis | Incentive proportional to % LUS sites converted (pro-rated); minimum conversion required to qualify |
| Maturity | 9th of the following month |
| Example | BTS Count=10, LUS converted=6, LUS converted%=60% → Incentive Payout=BDT 600 (60% of BDT 1,000). If converted%=43% → BDT 0 (below minimum) |
[/TABLE]
CHICOX09 — Site Monetization Weight by Market Type
[TABLE]
| Market Type | Site Monetization Weight | Incentive Amount (of BDT 10,000) | Notes |
| LUS market (CHICOX09) | 20% | BDT 2,000 | Active for LUS distributors — weighted at 20% |
| Non-LUS market | 0% | BDT 0 | No site monetization incentive for non-LUS distributors |
[/TABLE]
Note: This 0% weighting for Non-LUS means non-LUS MDOs get their full BDT 10,000 allocated to other KPIs (EV, SC, LSO, BSO/SSO), while LUS MDOs have 20% of their max reward tied to site conversion.

12. CHICOX09 Special Consideration — Weightage Structures
CHICOX09 is a special condition introduced from Jul25. It defines KPI weightages for the distributor CHICOX09, distinguishing between LUS market and Non-LUS market MDOs. The weightage structure changed between Epoch 3 and Epoch 4.
Structure A — Epoch 3 (Oct25, Nov25, Dec25)
[TABLE]
| KPI | CHICOX09 / Non-GA Mkt. (LUS) % | Incentive (LUS) BDT | CHICOX09 / Non-GA Mkt. (Non-LUS) % | Incentive (Non-LUS) BDT |
| EV Secondary | 35% | 3,500 | 35% | 3,500 |
| SC Primary | 15% | 1,500 | 15% | 1,500 |
| Active LSO | 15% | 1,500 | 25% | 2,500 |
| BSO | 15% | 1,500 | 25% | 2,500 |
| Site Monetization | 20% | 2,000 | 0% | 0 |
| TOTAL | 100% | 10,000 | 100% | 10,000 |
[/TABLE]
Note: For Non-LUS MDOs, LSO and BSO get higher weight (25% each) because Site Monetization drops to 0%. EV Secondary and SC Primary remain the same.
Structure B — Epoch 4 (Jan26, Feb26, Mar26)
[TABLE]
| KPI | CHICOX09 / Non-GA Mkt. (LUS) % | Incentive (LUS) BDT | CHICOX09 / Non-GA Mkt. (Non-LUS) % | Incentive (Non-LUS) BDT |
| EV Secondary | 35% | 3,500 | 45% | 4,500 |
| SC Primary | 15% | 1,500 | 20% | 2,000 |
| Active LSO | 30% | 3,000 | 35% | 3,500 |
| Site Monetization | 20% | 2,000 | 0% | 0 |
| TOTAL | 100% | 10,000 | 100% | 10,000 |
[/TABLE]
Note: BSO removed from the weightage table in Jan26 (replaced by SSO). Active LSO weight raised from 15% to 30% (LUS) and 25% to 35% (Non-LUS). EV Secondary Non-LUS weight raised from 35% to 45%. SC Primary Non-LUS raised from 15% to 20%.

13. Pro-Rated Calculation for Mid-Month New DDs
When a distributor (DD) is newly onboarded or activated mid-month, a pro-rated special consideration applies. All KPI achievements and incentive amounts are calculated proportionally for the active period only. This condition appears in most SRFs from Apr25 onward.
[TABLE]
| Condition | Achievements and incentives calculated on a pro-rated basis for the given active timeline |
| Modality Change | No other change in incentive modality — only the timeline and amounts are pro-rated |
| Format | DD Code, Region, Achievement Count Timeline (start–end dates), KPI-wise incentive amounts, Total Incentive Eligibility |
| Example (Jun25) | KHUJHE08 (Kushtia): Active 6/15/2025–6/30/2025 → EV Secondary 1,600 + SC Primary 800 + Bundle 1,867 + LSO 533 + BSO 533 = Total 5,333 |
| Example (Jul25) | SYLBRA04 (Brahman Baria): Active 7/7/2025–7/31/2025 → includes Site Monetization + GA |
| Example (Jan26) | RAJGAI07 (Bogura): Active 1/19/2026–1/31/2026 → VLR condition applies to GA |
| Applies To | All main monthly SRFs where a DD was not active for the full month |
[/TABLE]

15. DRC Exclusion Conditions
DRC (presumably a market segment or transaction type) is excluded from achievement calculations. The scope of exclusion expanded over time.
[TABLE]
| Epoch | Period | DRC Exclusion Scope |
| 1 | Apr25–Jun25 | DRC EV Secondary excluded only. DRC list shared by B2C. No DRC exclusion stated for LSO, BSO, Bundle. |
| 2 | Jul25–Sep25 | DRC excluded from: EV Secondary, GA, Total Bundles, Active LSO, BSO. (Explicitly stated for each KPI in Jul25+) |
| 3–4 | Oct25–Mar26 | DRC excluded from all KPIs. Achievement calculated 'without DRC & monobrand' stated globally at top of SRF. |
[/TABLE]
Note: Monobrand exclusion was added alongside DRC from Jul25 ('All Achievement will be calculated without DRC & monobrand'). Prior to Jul25, monobrand was not mentioned.
