1. Overview & Campaign Catalogue
This document consolidates all unique KPI conditions extracted from 21 SRFs in the Mixed Category folder. Unlike single-product folders, Mixed Category covers a wide range of campaign types, recipients, and commission mechanisms – from Device BP GA slabs to Monobrand TOS drives and SME ESO scale-up programmes.

Eligibility Cascade on Winning
When a DD achieves >=100% of the 5-day (or 7-day) C2C target, the following recipients become eligible for incentive: DD, DM (DD Manager), RSO Supervisor, and RSO of that winning DD.
Note: Both SRFs (17–21 Feb and 22–28 Feb) share identical conditions apart from campaign duration (5 vs 7 days) and agent list date.

Unique KPI Condition – Usage Gate
At least 95% of total GA must have more than BDT 5 in usage within 7th of the next month. This usage gate is unique and distinct from the standard 93% No-Play gate used in other campaigns.
Deno (Denomination) Recharge
The KPI measures C2C Deno Recharge activity at selected 'Tiger Retailer' outlets. The specific denominations and retailer list are both shared by B2C. This is a retail count target (number of retailers hit), not a total recharge amount target.
