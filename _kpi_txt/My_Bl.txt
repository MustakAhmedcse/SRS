Section 1: MyBL Standard (Regular – Prepaid) — BL Self-Channel
BL Self-channels (MyBL App) provide cashback to prepaid subscribers based on denomination and date. Commission is reported per retailer MSISDN 1967021235. Covers bi-monthly periods (1st–15th and 16th–end of month).
1.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]

| Periodicity | Bi-monthly: Period A = 1st–15th, Period B = 16th–end of month |

Commission is a fixed BDT amount per successful hit (transaction) for each denomination within a specific date and time slot. Multiple time slots may exist within the same date (e.g., premium rate 5 PM–7 PM and standard rate 7 PM–midnight).
