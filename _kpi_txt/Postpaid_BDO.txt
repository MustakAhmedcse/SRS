Section 1: BDO Variable Incentive (Regular Monthly)
Individual BDO (Business Development Officer) earns monthly variable incentive based on Gross Add (GA) + MNP acquisition performance under their own retailer code. Commission is slab-based — proportional to achievement between 75%–100% of target.
1.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | B2C Postpaid BDO (individual retailer code) |
| Commission Basis | GA + MNP count under BDO's own retailer code |
| Max Incentive | BDT 3,000 (from Jan26 with VLR condition added; see Section 9 for VLR variant) |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly |
| Report Slot | Disbursement |
| AIT | Applicable |
| Salescom to POS | No |
| Salescom to IN HOUSE | No |
| Maturity Date | 8th of following month |
| Data Source | IT SC, DWH, DMS, Channel Mapping |
[/TABLE]
1.2 Incentive Slab
[TABLE]
| GA+MNP Achievement % | Incentive Payable |
| Less than 75% | BDT 0 (no incentive) |
| 75% to 100% | At Actual (proportional up to BDT 3,000 max) |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| NOTE: 'At Actual' means the incentive is paid proportionally. Example: 90% achievement = 90% of BDT 3,000 = BDT 2,700. The BDO must achieve at least 75% GA target to qualify for any incentive. |
[/TABLE]
1.3 Eligibility Conditions
Specific Conditions
[TABLE]
| 1 | BDO must achieve at least 75% of monthly GA target (GA includes MNP). |
| 2 | Activation retailer type must be 'B2C_postpaid' in DMS. |
| 3 | Only eligible subscription IDs and option group bundles will be counted (see Appendix A). |
| 4 | Security deposit must be ensured on the same day as activation. |
| 5 | Security deposit must be equal to or greater than the bundle-wise minimum amount (see Appendix B). |
| 6 | Security deposit top-up can be done via i-top number list (to be shared) OR via BL Power App (any registered BL Power number). |
| 7 | Top-up for security deposit can be in one shot or multiple — but total must add up to required deposit. |
| 8 | For TK200 (non-bundle) packs, deposit amount must be extracted from DWH (Table: POSTPAID_COMMISSION_DATA_MONTHLY_DWH). |
| 9 | 98% ETSAF must be submitted by 7th of the following month by all retailer codes under his DDCODE. |
| 10 | Disconnection data: latest data up to report generation date (T-6). |
| 11 | AIT will be applicable on all commission amounts. |
| 12 | Data source for BL Power deposits: File bl_power_recharge_dwh_yyyymmdd.csv at /ccn1/downstream/export/daily/salescom. |
[/TABLE]

Section 8: M2 Survival Commission — BDO
BDO earns monthly commission based on whether customers acquired in month M0 generate any revenue in month M2 (3rd month = 2 months after activation). This is a retention-quality metric on top of acquisition.
8.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | B2C Postpaid BDO (individual retailer code) |
| Commission Concept | M2 Survival = M0 activations that generate any revenue in their 3rd month |
| Commission Basis | GA % achievement AND M2 revenue generating rate (both conditions must be met) |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly |
| Effective From | Nov25 (first version); Jan26 (revised with higher tiers and minimum target threshold) |
| AIT | Applicable |
| Maturity Date | 1st of following month (not 8th — different from all other BDO commissions) |
| Data Source | ED, IT BI, DWH, TABS, DMS, POS |
[/TABLE]
8.2 Commission Structure by Version
[TABLE]
| Version | GA Achievement Required | M2 Revenue Generating Rate Required | Commission (BDT) |
| Nov25 (original) | Min 50% of GA target (any BDO) | 80% or more of M0 GAs generate revenue in M2 | BDT 3,000 |
| Jan26 (revised) | 93% of GA target (min target ≥ 50) | 80% or more of M0 GAs generate revenue in M2 | BDT 3,000 |
| Jan26 (revised) | 100% of GA target (min target ≥ 50) | 80% or more of M0 GAs generate revenue in M2 | BDT 5,000 |
| Jan26 | Monthly target < 50 | N/A | NOT ELIGIBLE |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| IMPORTANT: Maturity date is 1st of following month (not 8th). M2 Survival commission for M0 activations is published 2 months after the activation month. Example: Nov25 activations → Jan26 M2 survival month → Commission published 1st Feb26. |
[/TABLE]

| RXXXXXX | Jan-2026 | 45 | 40 | 89% | Mar-2026 | 32 | 80% | NOT ELIGIBLE (target <50) |
[/TABLE]
8.4 Specific Conditions
Conditions
[TABLE]

Section 9: BDO VLR Active Incentive
BDO earns incentive based on a dual condition: GA achievement percentage AND VLR (Visitor Location Register) Active rate of their activations. Both conditions must meet minimum thresholds. Introduced from Jan26.
9.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | B2C Postpaid BDO (individual) |
| Commission Concept | Dual condition: GA% achievement AND VLR Active rate of activations |
| Max Incentive | BDT 3,000 |
| Commission Type | Proportional (At Actual) — commission = (GA%) × BDT 3,000 |
| GA Threshold | Must achieve at least 75% of monthly GA target |
| VLR Threshold | At least 75% of total monthly activations must be VLR Active |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly (from Jan26) |
| AIT | Applicable |
| Maturity Date | 8th of following month |
| VLR Data | VLR report shared monthly by Postpaid Team until Salescom can validate independently |
[/TABLE]
9.2 Dual Eligibility Conditions
[TABLE]
| Condition | Minimum Threshold | Notes |
| GA % Achievement | ≥ 75% | If <75%, BDT 0 — no incentive payable regardless of VLR |
| VLR Active Rate | ≥ 75% | At least 75% of monthly GA count must be VLR active |
| Commission Amount | At Actual × BDT 3,000 | Proportional to GA% if both thresholds met |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| IMPORTANT: Both conditions (GA ≥75% AND VLR Active ≥75%) must be met. If GA is ≥75% but VLR is <75%, no incentive is paid. The commission is proportional to the GA% (not VLR%). Example: GA = 95%, VLR Active = 79% → Commission = 95% × 3,000 = BDT 2,850. |
[/TABLE]
