Section 1: Postpaid GA Achievement with Daily Participation — BDO
BDO earns a fixed monthly bonus for achieving at least 93% of their GA target, provided they also maintain a minimum number of active participation days in that month. Two bonus tiers exist — higher bonus for more participation days, lower bonus for fewer. The participation day thresholds are adjusted each month to reflect the total working days available.
1.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | B2C Postpaid BDO (individual retailer code) |
| Commission Concept | Dual condition: GA % achievement ≥93% AND minimum monthly participation days |
| Commission Tiers | BDT 4,000 (higher tier) or BDT 2,000 (lower tier) — fixed, not proportional |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly (Jan26 onwards) |
| Reporting | Jan26 and Apr26 as Regular; Mar26 filed as Ad-hoc |
| Report Slot | Disbursement only |
| AIT | Applicable |
| Maturity Date | 8th of following month |
| Salescom to POS | No |
| Salescom to IN HOUSE | No |
| Data Source | ED, IT BI, DWH, TABS, DMS, POS; DWH table: POSTPAID_COMMISSION_DATA_MONTHLY_DWH |
[/TABLE]
1.2 Bonus Structure — by Month
The GA achievement threshold (93%) is constant across all months. Only the participation day thresholds change to reflect the number of working/calendar days per month:
[TABLE]
| Month | GA Achievement Required | Tier 1 (Higher Bonus) | Participation Days for Tier 1 | Tier 2 (Lower Bonus) | Participation Days for Tier 2 |
| Jan 2026 | ≥ 93% of monthly GA target | BDT 4,000 | ≥ 24 days | BDT 2,000 | ≥ 20 days |
| Feb 2026 | ≥ 93% of monthly GA target | BDT 4,000 | ≥ 22 days | BDT 2,000 | ≥ 18 days |
| Mar 2026 | ≥ 93% of monthly GA target | BDT 4,000 | ≥ 20 days | BDT 2,000 | ≥ 19 days |
| Apr 2026 | ≥ 93% of monthly GA target | BDT 4,000 | ≥ 24 days | BDT 2,000 | ≥ 20 days |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| KEY RULE: Both conditions (GA ≥93% AND participation days ≥ threshold) must be met to qualify for either tier. A BDO who achieves 95% GA but participates only 19 days in January gets BDT 0 — below the 20-day lower threshold. The participation day thresholds are set lower in shorter months (e.g., Feb requires only 18/22 days vs Jan's 20/24). |
[/TABLE]
1.3 Eligibility Logic
[TABLE]
| Scenario | GA Achievement | Participation Days (e.g. Jan26) | Commission Earned |
| Qualifies for Tier 1 | ≥ 93% | ≥ 24 days | BDT 4,000 |
| Qualifies for Tier 2 only | ≥ 93% | ≥ 20 days but < 24 days | BDT 2,000 |
| Does not qualify | ≥ 93% | < 20 days | BDT 0 |
| Does not qualify | < 93% | Any | BDT 0 |
[/TABLE]

Section 2: Postpaid Slab-wise GA Achievement Bonus — BDO
BDO earns bonuses based on achieving two within-month date-specific GA count targets. Each slab covers a 10-day window (1st–10th and 11th–20th), requiring 20 GAs per slab. Can earn both slabs (BDT 2,000 total) or just one. Missing the first slab does not prevent earning the second. Overall monthly GA achievement of ≥93% is also required.
2.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | B2C Postpaid BDO (individual retailer code) |
| Commission Concept | Within-month date-based slabs — two 10-day windows each requiring 20 GAs |
| Max Commission | BDT 2,000 (BDT 1,000 per slab × 2 slabs) |
| Overall GA Threshold | BDO must achieve at least 93% of monthly GA target to be eligible |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly (observed: Jan26) |
| Report Slot | Disbursement only |
| AIT | Applicable |
| Maturity Date | 8th of following month |
| Data Source | ED, IT BI, DWH, TABS, DMS, POS |
[/TABLE]
2.2 Slab Structure
[TABLE]
| Slab | Date Window | GA Count Required | Commission | Independence Rule |
| Slab 1 | 1st – 10th of month | 20 GAs within this window | BDT 1,000 | Can earn without Slab 2 |
| Slab 2 | 11th – 20th of month | 20 GAs within this window | BDT 1,000 | Can earn WITHOUT achieving Slab 1 |
| Both Slabs | Full month | 20 GAs by 10th AND 20 more by 20th | BDT 2,000 | Both earned if both windows met |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| INDEPENDENCE RULE: If a BDO misses Slab 1 (does not achieve 20 GAs by 10th), they are still eligible for Slab 2 commission (BDT 1,000) if they achieve 20 GAs between 11th and 20th. However, the overall 93% monthly GA target must STILL be met. |
[/TABLE]
2.3 Eligibility Matrix
[TABLE]
| Overall GA ≥93%? | Slab 1 Met (20 GAs by 10th)? | Slab 2 Met (20 GAs by 20th)? | Total Commission |
| Yes | Yes | Yes | BDT 2,000 |
| Yes | Yes | No | BDT 1,000 |
| Yes | No | Yes | BDT 1,000 |
| Yes | No | No | BDT 0 |
| No | Any | Any | BDT 0 (overall 93% not met) |
[/TABLE]

2.5 Key Differentiator vs. Section 1
[TABLE]
| Attribute | Section 1 (Daily Participation) | Section 2 (Slab-wise) |
| Commission Model | Fixed bonus based on total monthly participation days | Fixed bonus based on GA count within specific date windows |

Section 3: BL Prepaid and Postpaid GA Campaign — Monobrand CCR
Monobrand Customer Care Representatives (CCR) earn per-GA incentive across three achievement slabs for both Prepaid and Postpaid activations. Unlike BDO commissions, this campaign has a 3-slab per-GA rate structure, a recharge completion rate condition, AIT is NOT applicable, and the maturity date is 45 days after activation.
3.1 Campaign Overview
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Detail |
| Recipient | Monobrand CCR (Customer Care Representative) |
| Product Coverage | Both BL Prepaid AND BL Postpaid activations |
| Commission Type | Per-GA incentive — BDT amount per qualifying GA based on slab |
| Slab Basis | % achievement vs. individual CCR target AND recharge completion rate |
| Periodicity | Regular — Monthly (from Feb26 onwards) |
| AIT | NOT APPLICABLE (unlike all BDO commissions) |
| Maturity Date | 45 days after activation date (unique — all BDO types use 8th of month) |
| Max GA per Agent | Incentive eligible up to 100 GA per agent per month |
| Report Slot | Disbursement |
| Salescom to POS | No |
| Salescom to EV | No |
| Data Source | ED, IT BI, DWH, TABS, DMS, POS |
[/TABLE]
3.2 Incentive Slabs — Prepaid
[TABLE]
| Slab | Achievement vs. Target | 1st Recharge Completion Rate | Incentive per GA |
| Slab 1 | ≥ 93% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 1st recharge within 45 days | BDT 10 / GA |
| Slab 2 | > 93% to < 101% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 1st recharge within 45 days | BDT 30 / GA |
| Slab 3 | ≥ 101% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 1st recharge within 45 days | BDT 40 / GA |
[/TABLE]
3.3 Incentive Slabs — Postpaid
[TABLE]
| Slab | Achievement vs. Target | 2nd Recharge/Bundle Purchase Rate | Incentive per GA |
| Slab 1 | ≥ 93% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 2nd recharge or 2nd bundle purchase within 45 days | BDT 10 / GA |
| Slab 2 | > 93% to < 101% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 2nd recharge or 2nd bundle purchase within 45 days | BDT 30 / GA |
| Slab 3 | ≥ 101% | ≥ 95% of GAs must have 2nd recharge or 2nd bundle purchase within 45 days | BDT 40 / GA |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| POSTPAID RECHARGE CONDITION: For Postpaid, 'first recharge' in Prepaid is replaced by 'second recharge or second bundle purchase' within 45 days of activation. This means the customer must make a second payment (renewal or additional bundle) to validate the GA. No additional Postpaid bundle commission is considered from Feb26. |
[/TABLE]
