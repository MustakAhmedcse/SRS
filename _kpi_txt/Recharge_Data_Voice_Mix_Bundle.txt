This document consolidates all unique KPI conditions from 32 SRFs in the Recharge_Data_Voice_Mix Bundle folder. Campaigns run April 2025 – October 2025 across three recipient channels: DD Manager, RSO (Retail Sales Officer), and RSO Supervisor. Section 3: Eligibility Thresholds — Month-by-Month Evolution
All RSOs must meet a DUAL GATE: minimum EV% AND minimum Recharge (EV+SC)% vs. target. Both gates must be cleared simultaneously to qualify for any incentive. The incentive slab is then determined by the EV achievement range alone. Thresholds changed significantly month-to-month.

| May25 | 85% | 90% | None | Standard across all DHs |
| Jun25 | 90% | 95% | Central cluster: 85% EV / 90% Recharge | Two modalities: Except Central (higher) vs. Central cluster (lower) |
| Jul25 | 80% | 85% | Special DHs: 65% EV / 70% Recharge | Lower threshold for: DHKGOP09, DHKGOP10, DHKGOP11, DHKGOP08, DHKFAR04, DHKFAR08, DHKFAR09 |

| Oct25 | 70% | 80% | None | BL employee pool exclusion added; 4-category system continues; Manager 11,111/8,889 confirmed |

[TABLE]

Section 6: RSO 4-Category Ranking System (Sep25 & Oct25)
From September 2025, RSO incentives shifted from a simple EV-achievement slab to a within-DH ranking system. RSOs are scored and ranked within their DH, then assigned to one of four categories.
6.1  Category Assignment Rules
[TABLE]
| Ranking Basis | RSO scores calculated based on multiple KPIs; RSOs ranked within each DH. |
| Category Split | Top 25% of RSOs in each DH → Platinum; Next 25% → Gold; Next 25% → Silver; Remaining 25% → Bronze. |
| 50/50 Exception | If the DH achieves 100%+ on ALL KPIs, RSOs who individually reach 100% are split 50/50 into Platinum and Gold (not ranked quartile-based). |
| Platinum Prerequisite | Minimum 95% EV C2C Achievement required to qualify for Platinum. |
| Gold Prerequisite | Minimum 90% EV C2C Achievement required to qualify for Gold. |
| Silver Prerequisite | Minimum 80% EV C2C Achievement required to qualify for Silver. |
| Bronze | RSOs below Silver prerequisite or in the bottom 25% — no minimum EV threshold. |
[/TABLE]
6.2  Category Payouts
[TABLE]
| Category | Prerequisite (Min EV C2C) | Assignment Method | Payout (BDT) |
| Platinum | Min 95% EV C2C | Top 25% of DH (or 50% of 100%+ achievers if DH 100%+) | 8,000 |
| Gold | Min 90% EV C2C | Next 25% of DH (or 50% of 100%+ achievers if DH 100%+) | 7,000 |
| Silver | Min 80% EV C2C | Next 25% of DH | 6,000 |
| Bronze | No minimum | Remaining 25% of DH | 5,000 |
[/TABLE]
6.3  4-Category System vs. Prior EV-Slab System
[TABLE]
| Apr25–Aug25 System | Simple EV-achievement % slab: RSO earns based on how high their EV achievement % is. Slab bands and amounts defined in campaign modality table (shared by B2C). All RSOs clearing the dual gate can potentially earn the top slab independently. |
| Sep25–Oct25 System | Within-DH relative ranking: RSO's payout depends on their RANK within DH, not just absolute achievement. Even a 100% EV achiever can land in Gold (not Platinum) if the top 25% of the DH performs better. Absolute prerequisites (95%/90%/80%) still apply. |
| Key Difference | Old system: Absolute achievement → slab. New system: Relative rank within DH + absolute floor. Four fixed tiers (8,000/7,000/6,000/5,000) instead of variable slab amounts. |
| Bronze Tier (new in Sep25) | Bronze BDT 5,000 ensures all RSOs clearing the dual gate receive some incentive. Prior Apr25–Aug25 system: lowest slab RSOs may have earned less than BDT 5,000 (slab-dependent). |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| UNIQUE CONDITION — Relative Ranking: The 25/25/25/25 split means exactly 25% of RSOs in each DH land in each category (approximately). In a DH of 8 RSOs: top 2 = Platinum, next 2 = Gold, next 2 = Silver, bottom 2 = Bronze. The DH-100%-exception (50/50 split) rewards DHs where the whole team achieves 100%+ across all KPIs. |
[/TABLE]
