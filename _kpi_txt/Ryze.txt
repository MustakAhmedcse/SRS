Section 1: Overview & Campaign Taxonomy
RYZE is Banglalink's digital-first prepaid product assigned to Service Class 141. It is completely distinct from all other prepaid products; therefore RYZE GAs are excluded from all standard prepaid commission calculations and tracked separately under the RYZE team.
This document catalogues all unique KPI conditions extracted from 28 RYZE SRFs spanning April 2025 through April 2026 and beyond.

Section 2: Core Definitions & Eligibility Rules
2.1 RYZE Gross Add (GA) Definition
A RYZE GA is a successful RYZE Prepaid activation verified by:
FCD (First Call Date): Primary activation timestamp used for commission month attribution
FAD (First Activity Date): Used in some SRFs as an alternative activation date
Activation must occur within the defined promotion period
Only ETSAF-validated activations are counted (see Section 2.2)
MNP Port-IN connections also qualify as GA based on FCD/FAD
From Jun25 onwards for field force campaigns: RYZE App sign-up on the FCD is mandatory for the GA to qualify
IMPORTANT: RYZE GAs are completely separate from standard prepaid activations. Service Class 141 is dedicated exclusively to RYZE.
2.2 ETSAF (Electronic Telecom Subscriber Activation Form)
98% ETSAF compliance threshold applies universally across all RYZE SRFs
Deadline: All accepted/verified or submitted ETSAFs must be cleared by the 7th of the following month
Consequence of falling below 98%: The entire payout becomes zero (binary gate – not proportional)
Exception (Distributor Flat Commission): ETSAF is validated per-GA; non-compliant GAs are individually excluded rather than zeroing the entire batch
ETSAF 98% compliance is the most critical universal gate in all RYZE campaigns.
2.3 RYZE App Sign-Up Requirement
Mandatory from Jun25 onwards for field force campaigns (Device BP, Monobrand, CC, DSO, Supervisor, Retailers)
The customer must complete RYZE App sign-up on/before the FCD
GAs without App sign-up are excluded from commission even if ETSAF-valid
Pohela Boishak one-day campaigns (Apr26): Only GAs with completed App sign-up count toward slab achievement
Retailer campaigns: 100% of GAs on the list must have App sign-up (gate; zero if below 100%)
2.4 VLR (Visitor Location Register) – Variable Commission Modifier
VLR measures active subscriber presence in the network
Used as a payout modifier in CC and DSO Variable Commission campaigns
VLR ≥ 80%: Full 100% of earned variable commission paid
VLR ≥ 60% and < 80%: Prorated payout (proportional to VLR%)
VLR < 60%: Zero commission paid (despite achieving GA threshold)
Supervisor Variable Commission: VLR condition does NOT apply
2.5 Disconnection Policy
Data cutoff: T-6 (6 days before report generation date)
Any GA disconnected due to non-compliance is excluded from commission calculation
Disconnection between FCD and latest data up to T-6 is checked
Distributors: Non-compliant disconnections are discarded from the per-GA commission count
2.6 Advance Income Tax (AIT)
APPLICABLE: Distributor campaigns (all types) – as per Income Tax Law provisions and any government charges
NOT APPLICABLE: BSP CCR, Device BP, Monobrand field force campaigns (explicitly stated in SRFs)
CC, DSO, Supervisor: Not explicitly stated (handled through payroll/HR)
2.7 Mathematical Rounding Rule
Universal across all campaigns: Standard rounding – below 0.5 rounds down; 0.5 and above rounds up
Applies to all commission calculations, target achievement percentages, and prorated amounts
2.8 DMS Mapping
All distributor-retailer-MSISDN mapping uses DMS (Distribution Management System)
DMS mapping is used for commission month attribution of all distributor campaigns
Product codes in DMS: RYZ1001 and RYZ1001-ESIM
2.9 Minimum Recharge Condition
BDT 200 within 60 days of activation qualifies as a valid recharge
Counted cumulatively (any channel, OTT, bundles, add-ons all qualify)
Used in distributor ROI Support and slab-based GA campaigns as a follow-up condition
The 60-day window is absolute from activation date (not calendar month)
