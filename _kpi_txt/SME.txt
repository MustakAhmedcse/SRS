Section 2: Core Definitions
The following definitions are referenced across most or all KPI programmes in this channel.
[TABLE]
| Gross Add (GA) | A new prepaid/postpaid connection that meets the minimum First Recharge Condition (FRC) and SAF vetting requirements within the reporting period. |
| First Recharge Condition (FRC / GA Qualifying Recharge) | Apr 2025 – Sep 2025: ≥ 351 Tk recharge (I-TOP UP). Oct 2025 – Jan 2026: ≥ 350 Tk recharge (I-TOP UP). Feb 2026 onward: ≥ 350 Tk OR ≥ 251 Tk recharge; alternatively insurance bundle FRC 163 / 193 / 267 Tk qualifies. |
| SAF (Subscriber Acquisition Form) | a) DMS Post Date: form must be posted within 7 days of activation. b) Vetting Date: SAF vetting completed by the 14th of the month following activation. |
| Valid Recharge | I-TOP UP only. Excludes OTF (One-Time Free) recharges unless specifically stated. |
| Valid Usage | Any of: MOC, SMSMO, MTC, SMSMT, GPRS, MMS, Partial VAS usage (as per IN medication CDR), GPRS recharge, or bundle recharge. |
| Activation Date | FCD (First Call Date) is the standard activation reference. FAD (First Activity Date) and TABS are also referenced on forms. |
| T+1 Maturity | Commission data is matured and published 1 day after the reporting day (standard for most SME SRFs). |
| T-5 Disconnection | Disconnection status is checked against data up to 5 days before report generation. |
| DMS Post Date | Physical/digital SIM registration form submission: must occur within 7 days of activation. |
| Vetting Date | Regulatory/internal vetting cleared by 14th of the month following activation. |
| Survival Rate | Percentage of GA connections from a reference month that remain active (with qualifying recharge/usage) in subsequent months. |
| M1 / M2 / M3 | Month 1/2/3 after the GA month. Used in survival and repurchase KPIs. |
| Portfolio Revenue | Total revenue generated by an ESO's existing subscriber base (M1–M12 cohorts). Measured against a base period. |
| CAM | Channel Account Manager – the supervisor grouping used in Retention Programme tie-breaks. |
| ARPU | Average Revenue Per User = Total Usage Revenue ÷ Total GA count in the measurement period. |
[/TABLE]
Section 3: Gross Add (GA) Commission
The GA Commission is the primary acquisition incentive paid to ESOs for each qualifying new connection. The programme runs continuously but the recharge threshold has been revised multiple times.
3.1 Standard GA Commission Structure
[TABLE]
| Period | Min. Recharge Threshold | Total Payout | Achievement Slabs | MNP |
| Apr 2025 – Sep 2025 | ≥ 351 Tk (I-TOP UP) | BDT 4,000 | 90% target → 60% payout (2,400 Tk) 91–150% → payout at actual rate >150% → capped at 150% rate | Included |
| Oct 2025 – Jan 2026 | ≥ 350 Tk (I-TOP UP) | BDT 4,000 | Same slab structure | Included |
| Feb 2026 onward | ≥ 350 Tk OR ≥ 251 Tk (I-TOP UP) OR insurance bundle FRC 163/193/267 Tk | BDT 4,000 | Same slab structure | Included |
[/TABLE]
📌 Insurance bundles FRC 163/193/267 Tk qualify as GA from Feb 2026 onward – this is a significant expansion of the GA definition.
📌 MNP (Mobile Number Portability) connections count towards GA target in all periods.
3.2 GA Commission – Key General Conditions
[TABLE]
