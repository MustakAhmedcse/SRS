The SRFs span January 2026 through March 2026 across two distinct programme families: (1) Project Thunderstorm – Distributor Active SSO GA Campaign, and (2) RSO / RSO Supervisor Campaign on GA, Recharge, and LSO.

1.2 Channel & Recipient Types
[TABLE]
| Distributor | Distribution company responsible for managing retailers (SSOs) in selected Thanas. Earns incentive if the active SSO target per BTS is achieved. |
| RSO | Retail Sales Officer – direct field force under a distributor/CM. Earns incentive based on Recharge (EV+SC), GA, Strike Rate, and LSO KPIs. |
| RSO Supervisor | Supervisor overseeing multiple RSOs. Earns incentive based on the cumulative performance of all RSOs under them. |
| SSO (Active SSO) | Sales Support Officer / Retailer tagged to a specific BTS. An SSO is 'Active' for a BTS if they are tagged to that BTS AND achieve at least 2 FCD GA in that BTS. |
| LSO | Location-based Sales Officer metric – specific targets/definition shared by B2C team (appears in images in SRFs; exact LSO definition provided via supporting files). |
| BTS | Base Transceiver Station – the targeted cell tower location. Distributor incentive is BTS-level: each target BTS must have ≥2 Active SSOs. |
| Non-GA Market | Markets using CHICOX09 & CHICOX11 DH. For these markets, only EV Secondary + SC Secondary KPI is considered (no GA KPI). |
[/TABLE]
Section 2: Core Definitions
Key terms referenced across all SRFs in this channel.
[TABLE]
| Gross Add (GA) | A new FCD (First Call Date) connection activated during the promotion period, meeting all SAF, recharge, and product conditions. |
| FCD GA | Only First Call Date-based Gross Adds are counted. FAD (First Activity Date) and TABS are noted but FCD is the operative reference. |
| DRC GA | Distribution Retail Channel Gross Add. Excluded from Mar 2026 Project Thunderstorm campaign (explicitly stated in Mar26 SRF). |
| Active SSO | A retailer (SSO) who: (a) is tagged to a specific target BTS, AND (b) achieves at least 2 FCD GA within that same BTS in the measurement month. |
| BTS Tagging | BTS-wise SSO/Retailer tagging is shared by B2C for calculation. An SSO's GA only counts for the BTS they are tagged to – not for any other BTS where they may have made sales. |
| EV Secondary | Electronic Voucher recharge distributed from SR→Retailer (R2RT) and Distributor→Retailer (D2RT) and SUD12AGENT routes. |
| SC Secondary | Scratch Card secondary – Retail lifting as per DMS entry. |
| Recharge (Full Def.) | EV (R2RT + D2RT + SUD12AGENT) + DTR (Manual & Daily Commission from Salescom) + OTF (EV OTF + IRIS OTF) + SC (DMS Retail lifting). Net of withdrawals. |
| DTR | Daily Transaction Recharge – Manual and Daily Commission transferred from Salescom. |
| OTF | One-Time Free recharge: includes both EV OTF and IRIS OTF components. |
| C2C | Customer-to-Customer recharge from Digital Distribution Retailers (retailer list shared by B2C). Counted in Recharge KPI for RSO campaign. |
| Strike Rate | Retailer-level activation efficiency metric. Definition and targets shared by B2C team. |
| LSO Target | Location-based Sales Officer performance metric. Detail format shared via supporting files by B2C. |
| VLR | Visitor Location Register – measures active/present SIM cards. Used as a penalty gate in RSO/RSO Sup campaigns. |
| ETSAF | Electronic/Total SAF (Subscriber Acquisition Form) submission count. Measured as total against activations before disconnection. |
| RDB Mapping | Month-end Route Database mapping used for Retail-RSO-Supervisor tagging and Supervisor-CM/BP/DRC tagging. Applied at month-end for all calculations. |
| CHICOX09 / CHICOX11 | DH (Distribution Hub) codes identifying Non-GA Market areas where only EV+SC Secondary KPI applies (no GA component). |
| Slab 1 Target | The baseline (first) target slab used for RSO/RSO Sup categorization. Higher slabs exist for incremental incentive calculation. |
| Valid Recharge | I-TOP UP recharge for Data/Voice/Mix bundle upsell. For GA qualification purposes. |
| Valid Usage | Any outgoing call or internet usage in the calendar month. |
| T-6 | Disconnection reference: latest data up to T-6 days before report generation date. |
[/TABLE]

Section 4: RSO Campaign – GA, Recharge & LSO (Mar 2026)
Incentive programme for RSOs (Retail Sales Officers) measuring performance across three KPIs: Recharge (EV Secondary + SC Secondary), Gross Add (GA), and LSO. RSOs are categorized into Platinum / Gold / Silver tiers based on overall achievement.
4.1 RSO Categorization & Payout Structure
RSO category is calculated based on combined (EV Secondary + SC Secondary) & GA achievement. For Non-GA Markets (CHICOX09 & CHICOX11 DH), only EV+SC Secondary KPI applies.
[TABLE]
| Category | Achievement Condition | Payout (BDT) | Note |
| 🥇 Platinum | ≥ 100% achievement of Recharge (EV+SC Secondary) AND GA | 8,000 | Full target met |
| 🥈 Gold | ≥ 95% achievement of Recharge (EV+SC Secondary) AND GA | 7,000 | Near-full target |
| 🥉 Silver | < 95% achievement of Recharge (EV+SC Secondary) AND GA | 6,000 | Partial achievement |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| Categorization Target | Slab 1 target is used for GA & Recharge categorization (not higher slabs). |
| Non-GA Market Rule | For Non-GA Markets (CHICOX09/CHICOX11 DH): only EV+SC Secondary KPI determines category. GA is excluded from category calculation. |
[/TABLE]
4.2 Incremental Incentive – Above Slab 3 Target
For the Recharge (EV Secondary + SC Secondary) KPI only: if achievement exceeds Slab 3 target, an additional incremental payout applies.
[TABLE]
| Applicable KPI | Recharge (EV Secondary + SC Secondary) ONLY (not GA or LSO). |
| Trigger | Achievement must exceed Slab 3 target. |
| Incremental Rate | For every 10% above Slab 3 target: +20% additional payout on the allocated Recharge incentive. |
| Payout Calculation | 120% payout for reaching Slab 3 target + 20% additional for each 10% above Slab 3 = Cumulative incremental payout |
[/TABLE]
Worked Example:
[TABLE]
| Slab 3 Target | Actual Achievement | Achievement % | Payout Calculation | Total Payout % |
| 400,000 BDT | 440,000 BDT | 110% of Slab 3 | 120% (for Slab 3) + 20% (for 10% above Slab 3) | 140% of allocated Recharge incentive |
[/TABLE]
📌 The incremental incentive applies beyond Slab 3 only; within Slab 1–3, standard slab payouts apply. The slab table and target values are provided in a supporting file shared by B2C.
4.3 Growth Calculation Methodology
When comparing Last Month (LM) to Current Month (CM) performance, a day-count adjustment is applied to normalize for different month lengths.
[TABLE]
| Purpose | Adjusts raw LM numbers to account for different day counts between LM and CM. |
| Formula | Growth (%) = ((CM ÷ Day_CM) – (LM ÷ Day_LM)) ÷ (LM ÷ Day_LM) × 100 |
| Day Normalization | Monthly totals are divided by their respective day counts before comparison. |
[/TABLE]
Worked Examples:
[TABLE]
| LM Day Count | CM Day Count | LM Value | CM Value | Calculated Growth |
| 28 | 31 | 100 | 102 | -8%  (daily: 100/28=3.57 vs 102/31=3.29) |
| 28 | 31 | 120 | 121 | -9%  (daily: 120/28=4.29 vs 121/31=3.90) |
| 28 | 31 | 115 | 135 | +6%  (daily: 115/28=4.11 vs 135/31=4.35) |
[/TABLE]
📌 A Feb (28 days) → Mar (31 days) comparison with the same absolute number still results in negative growth due to the extra 3 days in March normalising the daily rate down.
4.4 VLR Penalty Structure
VLR (Visitor Location Register) performance acts as a penalty gate applied to the total earned incentive.
[TABLE]
| VLR Achievement | Penalty Applied | Final Payout |
| < 65% VLR | 100% penalty (full incentive forfeited) | 0% of earned incentive |
| ≥ 65% to < 85% VLR | Prorated deduction | Prorated on total incentive |
| ≥ 85% VLR | No penalty | 100% of earned incentive |
[/TABLE]
📌 VLR penalty is applied AFTER category-based payout is calculated. Even a Platinum-category RSO receives 0 payout if VLR is below 65%.

Section 5: RSO Supervisor Campaign – GA, Recharge & LSO (Mar 2026)
The RSO Supervisor (RSO Sup) earns incentive based on the cumulative performance of all RSOs under their supervision. The Supervisor's performance is aggregated from individual RSO results.
5.1 Supervisor Incentive Structure
Supervisor incentive is based on 100% vs. below-100% achievement of combined Recharge (EV+SC Secondary) & GA target.
[TABLE]
| Achievement Condition | Payout (BDT) | Notes |
| 100% achievement of Recharge (EV+SC) AND GA target | 8,889 | Full target hit by supervisor's team |
| Below 100% achievement of Recharge (EV+SC) AND GA target | 7,778 | Team partially achieves target |
[/TABLE]
[TABLE]
| Key Difference from RSO | RSO has 3 tiers (Platinum/Gold/Silver = 8,000/7,000/6,000 BDT). Supervisor has only 2 tiers (8,889/7,778 BDT) – a simpler binary structure with higher amounts. |
| No Silver Equivalent | There is no minimum threshold for supervisor payout. Below 100% always pays BDT 7,778 (unlike RSO Silver requiring <95%). |
[/TABLE]
5.2 Supervisor Performance Calculation
[TABLE]
| Aggregation Method | Supervisor performance = cumulative performance of ALL RSOs under that supervisor. |
| RSO Tagging Source | RSO-Supervisor tagging sourced from DMS (month-end). If not available in DMS, B2C provides the tagging. |
| Recharge Data Source | Supervisor's team Recharge performance is sourced directly from RSO Campaign_GA_Recharge_LSO_Mar26 (the RSO-level report). |
| Non-GA Market | CHICOX09 & CHICOX11 DH: only EV+SC Secondary KPI considered. |
| BL Employee Exclusion | Recharges at BL employee pool phone numbers are excluded. |
| Categorization | Slab 1 target used for GA & Recharge categorization (same as RSO). |
[/TABLE]
5.3 VLR Penalty – RSO Supervisor
