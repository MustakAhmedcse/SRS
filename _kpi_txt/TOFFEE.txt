Section 2: Core Definitions
[TABLE]
| Toffee App | Banglalink's OTT video streaming application. Commission is earned for driving new installations and registrations on customer handsets. |
| Successful Installation | Toffee app installed from Play Store (Android) or App Store (Apple) on a customer's mobile handset AND the customer registers using the agent's/retailer's unique Toffee referral code. |
| Unique Referral Code | A personalised code assigned to each Retailer, RSO, BSP CCR, Device BP, or Monobrand CCR. Only installations using this code are credited to that recipient. Referral codes and eligible lists provided by B2C/user team. |
| Eligible MSISDN | Only Banglalink Customer MSISDNs count. Installations on non-BL numbers are excluded. |
| New GA Installation | Toffee installation on a newly activated BL SIM. Allowed across all programme families. |
| Existing Customer Installation | Toffee installation on an already-active BL subscriber's number. Also allowed across all families. |
| Reactivated Toffee Base | A customer who previously had Toffee but uninstalled/lapsed. For Jun25 BSP/Device BP/Monobrand campaign only: users who have been inactive on Toffee for ≥30 days are eligible to be counted as new installations. |
| Retail Participation (RSO metric) | A retailer is 'participating' when they achieve at least 2 successful Toffee installations and registrations in BL customer MSISDNs using their unique referral code within the campaign period. |
| Conditional Commission | Jun25 BSP/Device BP/Monobrand only. A second commission component paid per installation IF the customer uses Toffee for ≥20 minutes within the first 7 days of installation. Paid on top of the Upfront Commission. |
| Upfront Commission | Jun25 BSP/Device BP/Monobrand only. Commission paid per installation regardless of whether the customer subsequently uses Toffee (no usage condition attached). |
| 20-Minute Usage Gate | Jun25 BSP/Device BP/Monobrand only. Customer must accumulate ≥20 minutes of Toffee usage within 7 days of installation for the Conditional Commission to be earned. |
| AIT Applicability | AIT (Advance Income Tax) is NOT applicable to Toffee commissions across all SRFs in this channel. Stated as: 'This is applicable for Sales Team' – meaning the AIT-exempt rule applies to the field force/CCR recipients, not to end customers. |
| TOS | Transfer of Ownership (Individual to individual) – referenced in the General Conditions of Aug25 Referral campaigns (BSP/Device BP/Monobrand). Not a KPI condition itself but a tracked dimension. |
| Single IMEI Rule | Monobrand Jun25 only: only one unique IMEI number per MSISDN is allowed for counting a Toffee installation. Prevents multiple installs on the same device from inflating counts. |
| Month-end RDB / Retailer DB | Month-end Retailer Database used for tagging and eligibility mapping. Applied to RSO and Retailer campaigns. |
| ETSAF QC | Electronic/Total SAF quality check condition. Referenced in RSO and Retailer SRFs. |
[/TABLE]

Section 5: TOFFEE Campaign – BSP CCR / Device BP / Monobrand CCR (Jun 2025)
Jun 2025 campaign for Alternate Channel recipients. Uses a tiered slab structure with two commission components: an Upfront Commission (paid per install) and a Conditional Commission (paid only if the customer uses Toffee for ≥20 minutes within 7 days).
5.1 Dual-Component Slab Payout Structure
[TABLE]
| Slab | # of Installations | Upfront Commission (per install) | Conditional Commission (per install, if ≥20 min usage/7 days) | Max Total (per install) |
| Slab 1 | 5 – 10 | BDT 10 | BDT 10 | BDT 20 |
| Slab 2 | 11 – 20 | BDT 15 | BDT 15 | BDT 30 |
| Slab 3 | 21 and above | BDT 20 | BDT 20 | BDT 40 |
[/TABLE]
📌 Both commission components are per-install amounts. Total monthly earning = (Upfront per slab × installs) + (Conditional per slab × qualifying installs with ≥20 min usage in 7 days).
📌 Minimum threshold is 5 installations to enter Slab 1. Fewer than 5 installs = no commission.

Section 7: Jun25 vs Aug25 – Alternate Channel Campaign Comparison
Key structural evolution from the June 2025 (slab-based) to August 2025 (flat-rate) Toffee campaign for BSP CCR, Device BP, and Monobrand CCR.
[TABLE]
| Parameter | Jun 2025 Campaign | Aug 2025 Referral Campaign |
| Commission Structure | Slab-based (3 slabs: 5-10, 11-20, 21+) | Flat rate (no slabs) |

| Upfront Rate (per install) | BDT 10 / 15 / 20 (slab-dependent) | BDT 20 (flat) |

| TOS Field | Not in General Conditions | Listed in General Conditions |
| Product Type | RYZE Prepaid | Toffee Mobile App (generic) |
[/TABLE]
📌 The shift from Jun25 to Aug25 simplifies the model significantly: the 20-minute usage gate is removed, slab complexity is eliminated, and every installation earns a flat BDT 20. The minimum threshold is also removed, lowering the barrier for entry.
